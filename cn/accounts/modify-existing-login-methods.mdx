---
title: "Modify existing login methods"
---

Log in to [Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/), then click **Login Methods**.

- To modify your login password, click **Change Password**. Currently, the email and password login method does not support modifying the email address.
- To add a new Web3 wallet, click **Add**. Up to 10 addresses can be added to your Cobo Accounts, and deletion is currently not supported.
- Once a Google Account is added, it cannot be replaced or removed.
- To add or remove a passkey, click **View Details**. Then, click **Create Passkey** to create a passkey.
