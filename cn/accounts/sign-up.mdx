## Use email and password for sign-up

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and switch to the **Sign Up** tab.
2. Enter your email address, then click **Continue**.

<img src="/images/cobo-accounts/of6u8wrbr2ASqXAtpvwa6IpVMHS7g1IiSJcvkEzDw92GFA2tBmn5KEgti8s9bwTgdxM1V63mavAxea6i8Psfzqg7elDhFY1mgA8_-dQmS2D-LEOQNIMKaBQk2RHG6dqkAREXUGIGoglOmbrJVSNyN-s.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

3. Set a password with a minimum of 8 characters, including at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.
4. Confirm your password, then click **Continue**.
5. Enter the six-digit verification code sent to your registered email, then click **Continue**.
6. After successfully verifying your email, you will be redirected to the Cobo Accounts dashboard. For detailed guidance on managing login methods and multi-factor authentications, see [**Manage login methods**](/cn/accounts/add-passkey-as-login-method) and [**Manage multi-factor authentications**](/cn/accounts/reset-google-authenticator).

## Use Google Account for sign-up

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and switch to the **Sign Up** tab and click **Google Account**.

<img src="/images/cobo-accounts/soLuJ7iMGhkKnO47y_pLjsVnmAl4PU0PPOd5FXvV8mYEL10xGahnLW4W38qopwSr-C2-uxsSuO7H8EqUy-Wuz3vnYjbE2KWDTFCIDnXX3FM7rKnVL3r0OGY4fhfNfCOT5-e8ocf2JJNAuyEtWyUvtQk.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

2. Follow the on-screen instructions and sign up using your Google Account.
3. You will be redirected to the Cobo Accounts dashboard. For detailed guidance on managing login methods and multi-factor authentications, see [**Manage login methods**](/cn/accounts/add-passkey-as-login-method) and [**Manage multi-factor authentications**](/cn/accounts/reset-google-authenticator).

## Use Web3 wallet for sign-up

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and switch to the **Sign Up** tab and click **Web3 Wallet**.

<img src="/images/cobo-accounts/soLuJ7iMGhkKnO47y_pLjsVnmAl4PU0PPOd5FXvV8mYEL10xGahnLW4W38qopwSr-C2-uxsSuO7H8EqUy-Wuz3vnYjbE2KWDTFCIDnXX3FM7rKnVL3r0OGY4fhfNfCOT5-e8ocf2JJNAuyEtWyUvtQk.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

2. Select your preferred wallet and sign a message within your wallet.

3. You will be redirected to the Cobo Accounts dashboard. For detailed guidance on managing login methods and multi-factor authentications, see [**Manage login methods**](/cn/accounts/add-passkey-as-login-method) and [**Manage multi-factor authentications**](/cn/accounts/reset-google-authenticator).
