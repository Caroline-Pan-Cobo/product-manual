## A transaction approval application
Use Cobo Guard to approve critical operations such as transactions and changes to your risk control strategies.

## A key share manager
Generate and back up your key shares securely, and restore them from iCloud when needed.

## A Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) method for Cobo Account
Use Cobo Guard as an MFA method to safeguard critical operations such as changing passwords.

## A passwordless login mechanism for Cobo Account
Unlock a passwordless login experience once you have configured Cobo Guard on Cobo Accounts.
