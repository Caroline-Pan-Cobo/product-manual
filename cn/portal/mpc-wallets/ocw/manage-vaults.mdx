Follow the steps below to navigate to your vault:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets**.
3. Select your vault.

Note: Only the vault admin has the permission to modify the MPC vault name, and export addresses and balances.

<img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/manage-mpc-vault.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

- **Export Addresses and Balances** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/export-address.svg" className="icon"></img>: Export details such as the list of addresses under your vault and their corresponding balances. The button will be visible only after the successful creation of the Main Group.
- **View Transactions** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View all transactions associated with your vault. The button will be visible only after the successful creation of the Main Group.
- **Manage Key Share Groups** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/manage-key-share-group.svg" className="icon"></img>: View root extended public keys associated with each vault. Manage Main Group, Signing Group and Recovery Group. For more information, refer to [Manage key share groups](/cn/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/manage-key-share-groups).
- **Edit Vault Name** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Modify the name of your vault. Each vault name must be unique within the organization and does not exceed a character limit of 30.
- **Search** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Quickly locate your vault by searching with the vault name.
- **Sort** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/icon_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Organize your vaults by sorting based on creation time, last activity time, and total balance.
- **Grid View** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/icon_grid.svg" className="icon"></img>: Display your vaults in a grid arrangement, with each entry containing information such as total balance, number of tokens, and the count of wallets in each vault.
- **List View** <img src="/cn/images/mpc-wallet/icon_list.svg" className="icon"></img>: Display your vaults in a linear format, with each entry containing information such as total balance, number of tokens, last activity time, and more.
