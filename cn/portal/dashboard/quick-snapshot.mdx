Get a quick snapshot of your total asset value as of the current time and the total deposits and withdrawals made over the last 24 hours.

- **Total Assets**: Your total asset value in USD as of the current time.
- **Total Deposit**: Total inflow in USD including all deposits of tokens, NFTs, and other assets, as well as gas refills from Gas Station over the last 24 hours.
- **Total Withdrawal**: Total outflow in USD including all withdrawals of tokens, NFTs, and other assets to both internal (Cobo Portal) and external addresses.
