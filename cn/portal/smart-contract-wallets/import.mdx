Import the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; you’ve just created to Cobo Argus using these simple steps:

1. Log in to [Cobo Argus](https://argus.cobo.com/#/login). If you have not signed up for a Cobo Argus account, see the Cobo Argus setup guide. (Link to Cobo Argus article)
2. Click Import Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;.
3. Select a wallet to connect.
4. Scan to connect your wallet.
5. Confirm on your wallet app (MetaMask/Rabby Wallet).
6. Select the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;s you want to import to Cobo Argus, then click Continue.
7. Enter a name for each Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; you want to import, then click Import.
8. Sign the message in your connected wallet.
9. Your Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; is now connected to Cobo Argus.
