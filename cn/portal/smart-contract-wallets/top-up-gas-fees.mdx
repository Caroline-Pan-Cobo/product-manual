title: "充值 Gas费"
sidebarTitle: "为 Delegate 充值 Gas费"
---

<Warning>中文版本正在建设中，敬请期待！</Warning>

1. Switch to the **Delegates** tab.
2. Locate the **Balance** column, and click the **gas icon** next to the delegate address you added.
3. Copy the deposit address to top up gas fees.
4. (Optional) If you wish to deposit from any of your wallets created on Cobo Portal, click **Deposit from other wallets**.
