title: "将 Delegate 添加到智能合约钱包"
sidebarTitle: "将 Delegate 添加到智能合约钱包"
---

<Warning>中文版本正在建设中，敬请期待！</Warning>


1. Click into a Smart Contract Wallet and switch to the **Delegates** tab.
2. Click **Add a Delegate**.
3. Select an address from either your Custodial Web3 Wallet MPC Client-Controlled Wallet to designate as your delegate.
4. (Optional) If you need a new address, click **Add a new delegate address**, select a wallet type to continue, and click **Create Wallet**.
