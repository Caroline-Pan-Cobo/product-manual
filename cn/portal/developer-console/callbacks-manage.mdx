Manage the callbacks in your Developer Console easily by clicking on the corresponding icons:

- **Register a Callback Endpoint**: Create a callback with predefined conditions for receiving alerts on Cobo Portal.
- **Edit**: Make changes to the fields of the selected callback URL.
- **Revoke**: Permanently disable all Cobo Portal system notifications through the selected callback URL.
- **Filter**: Tailor your view by callback URLs based on the status and type of events.
- **Search**: Quickly locate your callback URL by searching with the URL or event description.
