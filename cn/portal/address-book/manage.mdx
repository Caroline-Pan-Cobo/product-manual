sidebarTitle: "Manage Address Book"
---

View and manage the wallet addresses in your Address Book easily by clicking on the corresponding icons:

- **Edit**: Modify the networks and labels for your wallet address.
- **Delete**: Remove a wallet address from your Address Book.
- **Transactions**: View detailed transaction history associated with each wallet address.
- **View Operation Log**: Track and review detailed operation log associated with the wallet address, including who added the address, when it was added or modified, and more.
- **Filter**: Tailor your view by filtering wallet addresses based on networks or last modified time.
- **Sort**: Organize your wallet addresses by last modified time and alphanumeric order of labels.
- **Search**: Quickly locate your wallet address by searching with the address or label.
