On the **Select Payment Method** dialog, click **Select** for either **Fee Station** or **Cobo Pay**:
    - Fee Station is our recommended method for paying bills and purchasing plans. For more details, refer to [Fee Station](/en/portal/fee-station/introduction).
    - Alternatively, use Cobo Pay to pay with your crypto wallet or bank card. For more details, refer to [Cobo Pay](/en/portal/bills-and-payments/payment-methods#cobo-pay).
