---
title: "Modify existing login methods"
---

- To modify your login password, click **Change Password**. Currently, the email and password login method does not support modifying the email address.
- To add a new Web3 address, click **Add**. Up to 10 addresses can be added to your Cobo Accounts, and deletion is currently not supported.
- Once a Google Account is added, it cannot be replaced or removed.
- To add or remove a passkey, click **View Details** > **Create Passkey** or click the **trash bin icon** next to the passkey you want to delete.
- For more information on how to reset Cobo Guard, please refer to [**Reset Cobo Guard**](/cobo-accounts/manage-multifactor-authentication-methods/reset-cobo-guard).
