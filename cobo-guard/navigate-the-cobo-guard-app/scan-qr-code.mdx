title: Scan QR Code
---

Cobo Guard allows you to bind your Cobo Accounts with Cobo Guard simply by scanning designated QR codes. Cobo Guard then enables a passwordless login experience once you've configured Cobo Guard on Cobo Accounts. <font color="red">(Link to the [Configure Cobo Guard](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ppkUH9SawXkas-RLsI8eS2yRuB4SHrRcIb7guJUIMZU/edit#bookmark=id.ijwho76nnuc4) section under Cobo Accounts)</font>
