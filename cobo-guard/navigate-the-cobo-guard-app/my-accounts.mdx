---
title: My Accounts
---

My Account allows you to view and manage the Cobo Accounts bound to your Cobo Guard.
Access My Account by tapping <img src="/images/cobo-guard/common/menu-icon.png" /> -> **My Accounts**.

**Note**: You will not see the key share information if you are not a holder of any MPC key share.
