<u>**A transaction approval application**</u><br />
Use Cobo Guard to approve critical operations such as transactions and changes to your risk management strategies.

<u>**An MPC key share manager**</u><br />
Generate and back up your MPC key shares securely, then restore them from iCloud when needed.

<u>**A Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) method for Cobo Account**</u><br />
Use Cobo Guard as an MFA method to safeguard critical operations such as changing passwords.

<u>**A passwordless login mechanism for Cobo Account**</u><br />
Unlock a passwordless login experience once you've configured Cobo Guard on Cobo Account.
