### <u>**Deposit from external wallets**</u>

1. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
2. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you wish to deposit into.

### <u>**Deposit from another wallet within Cobo Portal**</u>

To learn how to deposit into your MPC Wallet, see [Transfer](/cobo-portal/transfer/introduction).

### <u>**Withdraw from MPC Wallet**</u>

To learn how to make a transfer from your MPC Wallet, see [Transfer from MPC Wallet](/cobo-portal/transfer/transfer-from-mpc).
