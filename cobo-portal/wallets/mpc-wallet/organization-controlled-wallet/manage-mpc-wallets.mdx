Manage your MPC Wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons:

### <u>**Addresses**</u>

- **Deposit** [insert icon]: Initiate a deposit into your MPC wallet address.
- **Withdraw** [insert icon]: Make a withdrawal from your MPC wallet address.
- **Create Address** [insert icon]: Generate a new unique receiving wallet address.
- **View Transactions** [insert icon]: View your wallet activity, including a detailed history of past and pending transactions.
- **View Transaction Policies** [insert icon]: View the transaction policies associated with your wallet - a pre-defined set of rules that dictate how transactions are handled and approved for every wallet, user, and transaction type.
- **Filter** [insert icon]: Tailor your view by filtering addresses based on network, asset type, last activity time, balance amount.
- **Sort** [insert icon]: Organize your addresses by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.
- **Search** [insert icon]: Quickly locate your mpc account by searching with the wallet name, wallet address, and label.
- **Edit Address Label** [insert icon]: Modify the label that is tagged to your address.
