To use Smart Contract Wallets, you will have to be a Cobo Argus user who has already set up Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; with Cobo Safe enabled.

Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; is a battle-tested leading multi-signature wallet on EVM chains.

Cobo Safe is a smart contract designed for on-chain permission and risk management of smart contract wallets (e.g. Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;).

Cobo Argus is an independent DeFi permission management product leveraging Cobo Safe's on-chain risk configuration capabilities. (Link to Cobo Argus section on product manual)
