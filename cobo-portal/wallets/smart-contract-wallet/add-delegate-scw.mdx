---
title: "Add delegate to Smart Contract Wallet"
sidebarTitle: "Add delegate to Smart Contract Wallet"
---
1. Click on a Smart Contract Wallet and switch to the **Delegates** tab.
2. Click on **Add a Delegate**.
3. Select an address from either your Custodial Web3 Wallet MPC Client-Controlled Wallet to designate as your delegate.
4. (Optional) If you need a new address, click on **Add a new delegate address**, select a wallet type to continue, and click on **Create Wallet**.
