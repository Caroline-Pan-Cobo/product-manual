---
title: "Top Up Gas Fees"
sidebarTitle: "Top Up Gas Fees for Delegates"
---

1. Switch to the **Delegates** tab.
2. Locate the **Balance** column, and click on the **gas icon** next to the delegate address you added.
3. Copy the deposit address to top up gas fees.
4. (Optional) If you wish to deposit from any of your Cobo wallets, click on **Deposit from other wallets**.
