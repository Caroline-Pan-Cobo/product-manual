View and manage the linked accounts in your Exchange Wallet easily by clicking on the corresponding icons:

- **Deposit** [insert icon]: Initiate a deposit into the account (note: deposits can be done on Main Account only).
- **Withdraw** [insert icon]: Make a withdrawal from the account (note: withdrawals can be done from Main Account only).
- **Transfer In** [insert icon]: Transfer assets into your Main Account from your Sub Account, or vice versa. Note that transfers between the same type of account are not allowed (e.g. from a Main Account to another Main account, or from a Sub Account to another Sub Account).
- **Transfer Out** [insert icon]: Transfer assets out of your Main Account into your Sub Account, or vice versa. Note that transfers between the same type of accounts are not allowed (e.g. from a Main Account to another Main account, or from a Sub Account to another Sub Account).
- **View Transactions** [insert icon]: View your accountâ€™s activity with a detailed transaction history, including past and pending transactions.
- **Unlink Account** [insert icon]: Disconnect your linked account(s).
- **Link Sub Account** [insert icon]: Add your exchange sub account(s) to your Main Account.
- **Filter** [insert icon]: Tailor your view by filtering accounts based on account type, asset type, last activity time, or balance amount.
- **Sort** [insert icon]: Organize your accounts by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.
- **Search** [insert icon]: Quickly locate your exchange account by searching with the account name.
