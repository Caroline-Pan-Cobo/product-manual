### <u>Move Assets In and Out of Multiple Exchanges from a Single Dashboard</u>
Exchange Wallet enables users to effortlessly transfer assets in and out of their exchange accounts, minimizing the risk of keeping them on exchanges. View, manage, and monitor your assets across different exchanges within a single dashboard, ensuring efficiency with a consolidated portfolio view.

### <u>Customize Security Settings Across Multiple Exchange Accounts</u>
Ensuring security across multiple exchange accounts can be a daunting task. Exchange Wallet simplifies this by enabling users to customize access controls and risk policies across your exchange accounts directly from one unified platform. Establish user roles, set withdrawal limits, whitelist addresses, and benefit from enhanced security measures designed to safeguard your digital assets.

### <u>Enhance Portfolio Oversight with a Consolidated View of All Your Exchange Accounts</u>
In addition to providing a consolidated view of the entire portfolio across various exchanges, Exchange Wallet offers real-time monitoring capabilities. Receive instant notifications for deposits, withdrawals, and other updates, and make informed decisions on your asset allocation and risk management.
