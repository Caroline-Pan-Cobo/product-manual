## What is Exchange Wallet?
Cobo Portal's Exchange Wallet is your one-stop solution for managing multiple exchange accounts effortlessly.

It brings all of your exchange accounts together in a single, user-friendly interface, providing a seamless experience for viewing, monitoring, and managing your assets across various exchanges.

Exchange Wallet makes it easy for you to transfer your assets between your exchange accounts and your wallets in just a few clicks - all without the hassle of logging in and out, and navigating through different exchange platforms.
