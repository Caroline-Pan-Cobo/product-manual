## Create Asset Wallet

Custodial Wallets (Asset Wallets) allow you to transfer tokens (initiate deposits or withdrawals). You may create multiple wallets according to your business needs. To set up your Custodial Wallets on Cobo Portal, follow these simple steps:

1. Log into Cobo Portal.
2. Click on the wallet icon (**Wallets**) and then select **Custodial**.
3. Click on **Create Wallet**.
4. Fill in your preferred **Wallet Name**, select **Wallet Type = Asset Wallet** and click **Create**.
5. Once successful, you will see the newly created wallet on your dashboard.
