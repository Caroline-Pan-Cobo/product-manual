## Create Asset Wallet

Set up your Custodial Wallet (Asset Wallet) on Cobo Portal by following these simple steps:

1. Log into Cobo Portal.
2. Click on the wallet icon (**Wallets**) and then select **Custodial Wallets**.
3. Click on **+ Create Wallet**.
4. Fill in your preferred **Wallet Name**, select **Wallet Type = Asset Wallet** and click **Next**.
5. Once successful, you will see the newly created wallet on your dashboard.

Note: You can create multiple new wallets of the same **Wallet Type**, depending on your business needs.
