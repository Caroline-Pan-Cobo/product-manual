View and manage the wallet addresses in your Address Book easily by clicking on the corresponding icons:

- **Edit** <img src="/images/3.png" width="20" height="20"/>: Modify the networks and labels for your wallet address.
- **Delete** [insert icon]: Remove a wallet address from your Address Book.
- **Transactions** [insert icon]: View detailed transaction history associated with each wallet address.
- **View Operation Log** [insert icon]: Track and review detailed operation log associated with the wallet address, including who added the address, when it was added or modified, and more.
- **Filter** [insert icon]: Tailor your view by filtering wallet addresses based on networks or last modified time.
- **Search** [insert icon]: Quickly locate your wallet address by searching with the address or label.
- **Sort** [insert icon]: Organize your wallet addresses by last modified time and alphanumeric order of labels.
