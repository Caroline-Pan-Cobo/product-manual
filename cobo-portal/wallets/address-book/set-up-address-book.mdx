Set up your Address Book on Cobo Portal by adding your wallet addresses using these simple steps:

1. Log into Cobo Portal.
2. Click on the wallet icon (**Wallets**) in the main menu on the left hand side and then select **Address Book**.
3. Click on **Add Address**.
4. Fill in the wallet address and network associated with the address (you can select 1 or more). Verify that the details are correctly entered.
5. (Optional) Add a label (brief note of up to 30 characters) to tag your wallet address for easy identification.
6. Click **Submit** to complete the addition of your wallet address. Once successful, you will find the wallet address in your Address Book.
