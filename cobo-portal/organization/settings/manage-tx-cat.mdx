---
title: "Manage transaction categories"
sidebarTitle: "Manage transaction categories"
---

The organization admin possesses the authority to customize transaction categories within the Cobo Portal. Admins have the capability to introduce new categories, ensuring an accurate reflection of the organization's diverse transaction types. Whether it is for reimbursements, DeFi investments, trading, employee salaries, bounties, or other financial dealings, this feature enhances the clarity and effectiveness of financial tracking and reporting.
