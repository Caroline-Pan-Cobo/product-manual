title: "Change your payment plan"
sidebarTitle: "Change your payment plan"
---

You can change your billing cycle or payment method at any time. To do so, follow the steps below:

1. Log into Cobo Portal.
2. Click the profile icon at the bottom left, and choose **Pricing Plans**.
3. Click **Bills** on the left-hand side navigation.
4. Click **Bill Management** on the top right, and then click **Payment Plans**.
5. Select the pricing plan for which you want to change the payment plan, and then click **Modify**.
6. Update the billing cycle if necessary.
7. Click **Subscribe** to set up an automatic renewal, or click **Pay** to make a one-time payment.
