View and manage the linked accounts in your Exchange Wallets easily by clicking on the corresponding icons:

- **Deposit**: Initiate a deposit into the account. Deposits can be done on Main Account only.
- **Withdraw**: Make a withdrawal from the account. Withdrawals can be done from Main Account only.
- **Transfer In / Transfer Out**: Transfer assets between your Main Account and Sub Account, or between different account types (e.g. between Spot Account and Contract Account).
- **View Deposit Address**: View the deposit wallet addresses associated with the selected asset type.
- **View Transactions**: View your wallet activity, including a detailed history of past and pending transactions.
- **View Transaction Policy**: View the transaction policies associated with your wallet - a pre-defined set of rules that dictate how transactions are handled and approved for every wallet, user, and transaction type.
- **API Management**: Navigate to the respective exchange to generate your API key and API secret key.
- **Refresh**: Update and synchronize your asset balances with the latest data for your linked account(s).
- **Unlink Account**: Disconnect your linked account(s).
- **Link Sub Account**: Add your exchange sub account(s) to your Main Account.
- **Filter**: Tailor your view by filtering wallets based on blockchain network, asset type, or balance amount.
- **Sort**: Organize your accounts by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.
- **Search**: Quickly locate your selected wallet or token holding, by searching with the wallet name/address or asset type.
