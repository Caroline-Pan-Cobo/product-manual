Follow the steps below to navigate to your wallet:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets**.
3. Select your vault.
3. Select your wallet.

Manage your wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons.
## Addresses

<img src="/images/mpc-wallet/manage-wallet-address.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

- **Create Address** <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/add-address.svg" className="icon"></img>: Generate a new unique receiving wallet address.
- **View Transactions** <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View your wallet activity, including a detailed history of past and pending transactions.
- **Deposit** <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Initiate a deposit into your wallet address.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_withdraw.svg" className="icon"></img>: Make a withdrawal from your wallet address.
- **Edit Address Label** <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Modify the label that is tagged to your address.
- **View Transaction Policies**: View the transaction policies associated with your wallet - a pre-defined set of rules that dictate how transactions are handled and approved for every wallet, user, and transaction type.
- **Search** <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Quickly locate your wallet by searching with the wallet name, wallet address, and label.
- **Filter**: Tailor your view by filtering addresses based on network, asset type, last activity time, balance amount.
- **Sort** <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Organize your addresses by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.
