## Deposit from external wallets

1. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
2. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you wish to deposit into.

## Deposit from another wallet within Cobo Portal

To learn how to deposit into your MPC Wallets, see [Transfer](/portal/transfers/introduction).

## Withdraw from MPC Wallet

To learn how to make a transfer from your MPC Wallets, see [Transfer from MPC Wallets](/portal/transfers/from-mpc-wallets).
