Follow the steps below to create an MPC wallet.

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets**.
3. Select the vault that you created in [Create an MPC vault](/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/create-mpc-vault).
4. Click **Create Wallet**.
5. Name your wallet, then click **Create**.
