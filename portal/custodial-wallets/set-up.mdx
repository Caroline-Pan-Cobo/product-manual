## Create an Asset Wallet

Asset Wallets are a subtype of Custodial Wallets, which allows you to transfer tokens (initiate deposits or withdrawals). You may create multiple wallets according to your business needs. To set up your Custodial Wallets on Cobo Portal, follow these simple steps:

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click the wallet icon (**Wallets**) and then select **Custodial Wallets**.
3. Click **Create Wallets**.
4. Fill in your preferred **Wallet Name** (up to 30 characters), select **Asset Wallet** as **Wallet Type**, then click **Create**.
5. Once successful, you will see the newly created wallet on your dashboard.
