1. Once you have filled the required information, review both the estimated fee and the estimated amount to receive. Click **Submit**.
   <img src="/images/transfers/transaction-fees.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>
2. Approve the request on your Cobo Guard. If it is a new address not found in your Address Book or any of your Cobo wallets, you will be prompted to add the address to your Address Book.
