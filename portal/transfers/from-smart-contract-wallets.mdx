To make a transfer from Smart Contract Wallets (Safe\{Wallet\}), click <img src="/images/transfers/transfer-button.png" className="icon"></img> in the main menu on the left hand side.

<img src="/images/transfers/transfer-button-location.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>
 

Then, fill in the required information as follows:

1. Select the token type for the transfer. Select the token type for the transfer. Selection is limited to tokens included in your organization's subscription package. To enable a new token type (fees apply), click **Not enabled**.
2. In the drop-down menu under "From", select **Smart Contract Wallet (Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;)**.
3. To find your wallet, select it from the drop-down menu or search by the address, Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; name or label. Smart Contract Wallets (Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;) created via both Cobo Portal and WaaS 2.0 API will be listed.
4. Select a Delegate address (abbreviated as Delegate). For more information on Delegates, see [Manage delegate addresses and transfer whitelists](/portal/smart-contract-wallets/delegate-intro).
    - If you have not added any Delegates to the Smart Contract Wallet (Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;) yet, please click **Add delegates**.
    - If the selected Delegate lacks gas tokens, please follow the on-screen instructions to top up gas fees for the Delegate first.
    <img src="/images/transfers/add-delegate.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>
5. Select a "To" address from your Address Book or a wallet on Cobo Portal, or manually input an external address. Note that for Smart Contract Wallets (Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;), the 'Toâ€™ address must be an on-chain, whitelisted address. For more information, see [Add transfer whitelists](/portal/smart-contract-wallets/transfer-whitelists).
6. Enter the transfer amount.
7. Review the transaction cost. Network fees will be displayed in 3 tiers based on transaction speeds: slow, standard, and fast.
7. To customize the fee, click **Customize**.
8. The fields will be auto populated based on the transaction speed you have selected. You may customize the gas price and gas limit based on your specific requirements.
    - The fee will be covered by the Delegate.
    - The estimated fee is calculated by multiplying the max fee by the gas limit: (max fee * gas limit). See [provided table under EIP 1559](/portal/transfers/from-mpc-wallets#eip1559) for definition of the parameters.
    - If the Delegate lacks sufficient gas tokens, click **Top up to Delegate** to deposit the required gas tokens.
