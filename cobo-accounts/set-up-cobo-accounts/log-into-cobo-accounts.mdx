#### Use Cobo Guard for login

1) Switch to the **Log In** tab.
2) Click **Guard**.
3) Ensure that your Cobo Guard is updated to the latest version.
4) Use your Cobo Guard to scan the QR code.
5) Select an account in your Cobo Guard and click **Log In**.

#### Use email and password for login

1) Switch to the **Log In** tab.
2) Enter your email address and password. Click **Log In**.

#### Use Google Account for login

1) Switch to the **Log In** tab.
2) Click **Google Account**.

#### Use passkeys for login

Note: Passkeys are an easier and more secure alternative to passwords. Once you create a passkey, you can use it for logging into Cobo Accounts through fingerprint, face scan, or screen lock (PIN code). See [**Manage login methods**](/cobo-accounts/manage-login-methods) for instructions on how to add passkeys as a login method.

1) Switch to the **Log In** tab.
2) Click **Passkey**.
3) Use your Passkey to confirm your identity.

#### Use Web3 wallets for login

1) Switch to the **Log In** tab.
2) Click **Web3 Wallet**.
3) Sign a message in your wallet.
