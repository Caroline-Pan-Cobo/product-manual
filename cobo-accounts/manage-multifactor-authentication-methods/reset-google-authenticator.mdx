---
title: "Reset Google Authenticator"
---

_If your old Google Authenticator is no longer accessible, please email custodyservice@cobo.com for manual verification and reset. Otherwise, follow the steps below to reset your Google Authenticator._

1) Log in to Cobo Accounts and click on **MFA Methods** in the main menu on the left hand side.
2) Click on **Reset** next to Google Authenticator.
3) Enter the 6-digit code on your old Google Authenticator.
4) Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your new Google Authenticator.
