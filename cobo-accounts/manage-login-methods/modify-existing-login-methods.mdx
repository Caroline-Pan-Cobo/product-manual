---
title: "Modify existing login methods"
---

- To modify your login password, click on **Change Password**. Currently, the email and password login method does not support modifying the email address.
- To add a new Web3 address, click on **Add**. Up to _10_ addresses can be added to your Cobo Accounts, and deletion is currently not supported.
- Once a Google Account is added, it _cannot_ be replaced or removed.
- To add or remove a passkey, click on **View Details**. Then, click on **Create Passkey** or click on the **trash bin icon** next to the passkey you want to delete.
- For more information on how to reset Cobo Guard, please refer to the **Manage multi-factor authentications** section.
