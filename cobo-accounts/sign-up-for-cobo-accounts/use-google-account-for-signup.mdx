---
title: "Use Google Account for signup"
---
1) Switch to the **Sign Up** tab and click on **Google Account**.
2) Select the Google Account you want to use to sign in to Cobo Accounts.
3) You will be redirected to the Cobo Accounts dashboard. For detailed guidance on managing login methods and multi-factor authentications, please refer to the **Manage login methods** and **Manage multi-factor authentications sections**.
