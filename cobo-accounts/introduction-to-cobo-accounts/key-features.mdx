---
title: "Key features"
---
### <u>Unified login and registration system</u>
Use a single set of login credentials to access most Cobo products, such as Cobo Portal, Argus and more. Enjoy a hassle-free signup and login experience, without the complexities of managing multiple accounts.

### <u>Flexible registration and login methods</u>
Choose from five convenient registration and login methods: Google Account, email address, Web3 address, passkey and Cobo Guard. Looking ahead, Cobo Accounts will integrate additional login and registration methods that tailor to your preferences.

### <u>Diverse multi-factor authentication methods</u>
Currently, three MFA methods are supported: Google Authenticator, security key, and Cobo Guard. Your MFA methods will be applied across most Cobo products by default, ensuring a consistent identity verification process and eliminating the need for repeated configurations.

### <u>All-in-one account dashboard</u>
View, add/remove, and modify your login and MFA methods easily through the Cobo Accounts dashboard. Gain a comprehensive overview of the services you use and the data stored in your Cobo Accounts.
