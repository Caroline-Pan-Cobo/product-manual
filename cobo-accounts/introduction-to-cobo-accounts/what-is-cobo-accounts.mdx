---
title: "What is Cobo Accounts"
---
Welcome to Cobo Accounts, a unified login system designed by Cobo to serve its suite of products, including Cobo Portal, Argus, and more.

Registration for Cobo Accounts is a straightforward process, offering users the flexibility to sign up through their Google Account, email address, or a Web3 address. Upon successful registration, users can access a comprehensive suite of Cobo products using a single set of credentials. The data housed within Cobo Accounts is maintained in an independent enclave, adhering to the highest standards of security and privacy.
