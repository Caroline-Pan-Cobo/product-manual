## Use email and password for login

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and switch to the **Log In** tab.
2. Enter your email address and password, then click **Log In**.

## Use Cobo Guard for login

<Note>[Cobo Guard](/en/guard/introduction) is a transaction approval application developed by Cobo that allows you to authorize transactions anytime, anywhere, and securely store your MPC key shares. For more information on how to configure Cobo Guard as a login method, see [Set up multi-factor authentication methods](/en/accounts/set-up-mfa).</Note>

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and switch to the **Log In** tab.
2. Click **Guard**.

<img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/-srHe9u4-CKdI4g1oFtV60EcPvltUBmBwoK5ht4oUjZqjrgWOFehBeMAi1LbkhK_nQhCocBtGJ3KQPeRULnFwg5w1-q0OMOg3nrYu3fVPxrL3cIvBC5ZfMFZTDleY0YsDtHRrRGGVyHtLqAnvtDhfns.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

3. Ensure that your Cobo Guard is updated to the latest version.
4. Use your Cobo Guard to scan the QR code.

5. Select an account in your Cobo Guard and click **Log In**.

## Use passkey for login

<Note>Passkeys are an easier and more secure alternative to passwords. Once you create a passkey, you can use it for logging into Cobo Accounts through fingerprint, face scan, or screen lock (PIN code). For more information on how to add passkeys as a login method, see [Add passkeys as new login method](/en/accounts/add-passkey-as-login-method).</Note>

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and switch to the **Log In** tab.
2. Click **Passkey**.

<img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/-srHe9u4-CKdI4g1oFtV60EcPvltUBmBwoK5ht4oUjZqjrgWOFehBeMAi1LbkhK_nQhCocBtGJ3KQPeRULnFwg5w1-q0OMOg3nrYu3fVPxrL3cIvBC5ZfMFZTDleY0YsDtHRrRGGVyHtLqAnvtDhfns.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

3. Use your Passkey to confirm your identity.
