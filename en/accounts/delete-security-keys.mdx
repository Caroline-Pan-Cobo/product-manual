---
title: "Delete security keys"
---

1. Log in to [Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/), then click **MFA Management** in the main menu on the left-hand side.
2. Click **View Details** next to Security Key.

1. Click <img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/pBtfB_6WRBfwcEIz1gcplIN-kdZwzvFRhfhX0ag-rpb4FFQYvbhSsA8aFmSBG8b61_6-9vZu9SulRIhPawPZxmND_Y_lZdhevEcEPP_YCg68wjAVtPGATgnY3D6Md3xwI_8Q5c2llDRxaSobBBXyag8-20240419120534443.svg" className="icon"/> next to the security key you want to delete.
2. Complete an MFA verification. Your security key is now successfully deleted.
