---
title: "Reset Google Authenticator"
---

<Note>If your old Google Authenticator is no longer accessible, please email [help@cobo.com](mailto:help@cobo.com) for manual verification and reset. Otherwise, follow the steps below to reset your Google Authenticator.</Note>

1. Log in to [Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/), then click **MFA Management** in the main menu on the left-hand side.
2. Click **Modify** next to **Google Authenticator**.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your new Google Authenticator.
