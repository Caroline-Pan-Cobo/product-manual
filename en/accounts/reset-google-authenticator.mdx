---
title: "Reset Google Authenticator"
lang: "en"
description: "Learn how to reset Google Authenticator for your Cobo Accounts, ensuring continued access and security for your digital assets."
---

If you want to reset your Google Authenticator for Cobo Accounts due to device loss or replacement, follow these steps:

1. Log in to [Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/), then click **MFA Management** tab.
2. Click **Modify** next to **Google Authenticator** to display the dialog as the following screenshot shows.

    <img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/_xhiQMOa7LNMh0f9QR3LCIVTSncU4VZIUcCZj5aCUP7mxmw2RaMN9mmwDwdp8mTyzWH063WyZPEcGKJEX_1sAUxBz6aMm5CVGUigupUkl3QYa0ci30dQ_C3RGL0KdVFpg7cobueWhNoxsdGdC-s23Mw.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="On-screen instructions for resetting Google Authenticator" />

3. Open Google Authenticator on your new device and scan the QR code, enter the six-digit code from Google Authenticator on the dialog, and click **Submit**.

4. On the Choose MFA method dialog, click **Google Authenticator** if your old Google Authenticator device is still accessible, otherwise click **Cobo Guard**.

    <img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/choose-mfa-method.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The Choose MFA method dialog" />

    - If you choose **Google Authenticator**, enter the six-digit code from old Google Authenticator device.

    <img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/reset-with-old-ga.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The screenshot of using old Google Authenticator device to reset" />

    - If you choose **Cobo Guard**, open your Cobo Guard and approve the modify-MFA request. Then click **Authentication completed**.

    <img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/reset-with-cobo-guard.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The screenshot of using Cobo Guard to reset" />

    <img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/cobo-guard-request.png" className="screenshot_modal" alt="The screenshot of Cobo Guard request" />



Now your Google Authenticator has been reset. You can use the new device to log in to Cobo Accounts.
