Welcome to Cobo Accounts, the unified login system designed by Cobo to serve its suite of products in [Cobo Portal](/en/portal/introduction).

<img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/lmj06pNK0IJPiSdTVKbGmNoeMQ-4mlCwbxTAhgxbHFOVAg1OJUznbSp6Tofbykm1MQBA1EVZaVctKyfBYheE-8TZfe8UkZ41dwO6nx6F9-vVYl5BQeO-P3u-yOaniN6p5_uw1Y91dSNosfu-zKCT3ko.svg" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

[Registration for Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/) is a straightforward process, offering users the flexibility to sign up through their email addresses, Google Accounts, or Web3 wallets. Upon successful registration, users can access a comprehensive suite of Cobo products using a single set of credentials. The data housed within Cobo Accounts is maintained in an independent enclave, adhering to the highest standards of security and privacy.
