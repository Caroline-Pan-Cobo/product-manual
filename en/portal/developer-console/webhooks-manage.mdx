Manage the webhook endpoints in your Developer Console easily by clicking on the corresponding buttons or icons:

- **Register Webhook Endpoint**: Register a webhook endpoint with predefined conditions for receiving alerts on Cobo Portal.
- **Edit** <img src="/en/images/developer-console/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Make changes to the fields of the selected webhook endpoint.
- **Revoke** <img src="/en/images/developer-console/icon_revoke.svg" className="icon"></img>: Permanently disable all Cobo Portal system notifications through the selected webhook URL.
- **Filter** <img src="/en/images/developer-console/icon_filter.svg" className="icon"></img>: Tailor your view by webhook endpoints based on the status and type of events.
- **Search** <img src="/en/images/developer-console/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Quickly locate your webhook endpoint by searching with the URL or event description.
