Manage the Webhooks in your Developer Console easily by clicking on the corresponding icons:

- **Create a Webhook Endpoint**: Create a webhook with predefined conditions for receiving alerts  on Cobo Portal.
- **Edit**: Make changes to the fields of the selected webhook endpoint.
- **Revoke**: Permanently disable all Cobo Portal system notifications through the selected webhook URL.
- **Filter**: Tailor your view by webhook endpoints based on the status and type of events.
- **Search**: Quickly locate your webhook endpoint by searching with the URL or event description.
