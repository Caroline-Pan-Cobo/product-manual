This tab provides detailed information about the Cobo Portal Apps developed by members of your organization. 

It includes metrics such as the number of organizations that have installed the app, the total number of users, the cumulative number of times these organizations have launched the app, page views, and unique visitors. 

You can view this data for all organizations or filter it to see metrics specific to a particular organization.
