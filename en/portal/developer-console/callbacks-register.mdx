Register a callback endpoint following these steps:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. On the left navigation menu, click **Developer** > **Callbacks**.
3. Click **Register Callback Endpoint**.
4. Enter the callback URL.
5. Enter a description for the callback (maximum 30 characters).
6. Click **Register**.
