Register a callback endpoint following these steps:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click the Developer icon <img src="/en/images/common/icon_developer.svg" className="icon" alt="the Developer icon" /> in the main menu.
3. Click the **WaaS 2.0** tab and then switch to the **Callbacks** tab.
4. Click **Register Callback Endpoint**.
5. Enter the callback URL.
6. Enter a description for the callback (maximum 30 characters).
7. Click **Register**.
