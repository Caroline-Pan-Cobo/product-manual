You can edit each governance policy to enforce customized approval rules. Below is a list of approval rules you can choose from for each governance policy.


<table class="table">
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Approval Rule</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Auto Rejection</strong></td>
      <td>Operations are rejected immediately without needing approvals.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Auto Approval</strong></td>
      <td>Operations are processed immediately without needing approvals.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Approval Quorum</strong></td>
      <td>Select a list of approvers and set up a T out of N approval quorum. (e.g. 2 out of 3 approvers must approve for operations to be processed). <br/> <br/> Approvers can either be specific users or user roles.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Customized Workflow</strong></td>
      <td>Create a customized workflow to enhance the flexibility of your action strategies.</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

To edit a governance policy, follow these steps:

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click the profile icon at the bottom left corner, and choose **Organization**.
3. Click the **Governance Policies** tab.

    <img src="/en/images/organization/overview.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The Governance Policies tab" />

4. Hover over the more icon <img src="/en/images/common/icon_more.svg" className="icon" alt="the more icon"></img> in the **Action** column and select <img src="/en/images/common/icon_edit.svg" className="icon" alt="the edit icon"></img> **Edit** to open the **Edit Approval Rule** pane.

    <img src="/en/images/organization/edit.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The Edit Approval Rule pane" />
    
5. Update the approval rule.
6. Click **Save**.
