Description: "Monitor approval notifications in Cobo Portal to stay updated on the status of your requests and enhance operational oversight."
---

You can conveniently review approval notifications to monitor the status of each approval request you have initiated or been designated as an approver. To access these notifications, click **Notice** > **Approvals** on the Cobo Portal.
