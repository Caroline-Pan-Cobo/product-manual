Click on the corresponding icons to manage your organization members:

- **Invite Members**: Add new members to your organization by sending an invitation to their email address.
- **Resend Email**: Resend the email invitation to the selected member.
- **Edit Role**: Modify the role of the member.
- **Delete**: Remove the member from your organization.
- **Freeze**: Temporarily restrict the member's access to the organization account's features.
- **Unfreeze**: Restore the member's access to the organization account's features.
- **Cancel**: Cancel the invitation.
- **Submit for Approval**: Submit the request to add a new member to the organization's admin for their approval.
- **Filter**: Tailor your view by filtering members based on name, status, role, or last activity time.
