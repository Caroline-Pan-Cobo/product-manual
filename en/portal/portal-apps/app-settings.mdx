This article describes how to update the settings of optional permissions and wallet scopes after you've installed a Cobo Portal App.

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).

2. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the apps button"></img> in the main menu on the left-hand side.

3. In the **All Apps** section or the **Installed Apps** section, click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_advanced.svg" className="icon" alt="the settings icon"></img> on an app to open the **App Management** page.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/app-management.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The App Management page" />

4. On the **App Settings** tab of the **App Management** page, you can view the required and optional permissions and the applicable wallet scope you've selected during app installation.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/app-settings.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The App Settings page" />

5. To update the selected optional permissions and the wallet scope, click **Edit** to open the **Edit App Settings** dialog.

6. On the **Edit App Settings** dialog, update the settings and click **Submit**.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/edit-settings.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The Edit Settings dialog" />

7. On the confirmation dialog, click **Confirm** to proceed.
    <Note>Depending on the <a href="/en/portal/organization/governance-intro">governance policies</a> of your <a href="/en/portal/organization/introduction">organization</a>, the settings update might require approval from your organization admin. When you click <b>Confirm</b>, your organization <a href="/en/portal/organization/roles-and-permissions">admin</a> might receive an approval request for the installation.</Note>

8. You can navigate to the **Apps** or app overview page and hover over the **Pending Approval** button or the clock icon <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/icon-pending.png" className="icon" alt="the clock icon"></img> to view the approval timeline. Once the approval is confirmed, the app settings will be updated.
