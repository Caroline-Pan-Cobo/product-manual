Leveraging the capabilities of the [WaaS 2.0 API](https://www.cobo.com/developers/v2/guides/overview/introduction), Cobo collaborates with various third-party providers to build a robust business application ecosystem, Cobo Portal Apps, enriching the way users invest, trade, and manage their digital assets.

You can conveniently explore and manage Cobo Portal Apps by clicking <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/icon-apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the apps button"></img> on Cobo Portal.

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/apps-overview.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Screenshot of Cobo Portal Apps" />

## Key features

### Comprehensive apps by Cobo
Cobo develops a wide array of Cobo Portal Apps, providing digital asset services such as off-exchange settlement (<a href="superloop/introduction">SuperLoop</a>), on-chain digital asset management solutions built on [Smart Contract Wallets](/en/portal/smart-contract-wallets), staking, and restaking services in one centralized location.

### Developer collaboration
Through the [WaaS 2.0 API](https://www.cobo.com/developers/v2/guides/overview/introduction), Cobo facilitates collaboration with third-party developers to expand the range of available Cobo Portal Apps continuously.

### Seamless integration
Cobo Portal Apps integrate seamlessly with the [Cobo Portal](/en/portal/introduction) ecosystem, providing a unified experience for discovering, installing, and managing applications.
