Leveraging the capabilities of the [WaaS 2.0 API](https://www.cobo.com/developers/v2/guides/overview/introduction), Cobo collaborates with third-party developers to build a robust business application ecosystem through Cobo Portal Apps, enriching the way users invest, trade, and manage their digital assets.

You can easily explore and manage Cobo Portal Apps by clicking <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the apps button"></img> on Cobo Portal.

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/apps-overview.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Screenshot of Cobo Portal Apps" />

## Key features

### Comprehensive apps developed by Cobo
Cobo develops a wide array of Cobo Portal Apps, providing digital asset services such as off-exchange settlement (<a href="/en/apps/superloop/introduction">SuperLoop</a>), on-chain digital asset management solutions built on [Smart Contract Wallets](/en/portal/smart-contract-wallets), as well as staking and restaking services in one centralized location.

### Innovative apps developed by third-party developers
Through the WaaS 2.0 API, Cobo facilitates collaboration with third-party developers to expand the range of available Cobo Portal Apps continuously. Developers can publish these applications to all Cobo Portal users or use them privately. For more information, see [Build your own apps](/en/portal/portal-apps/build).

### Seamless integration
Cobo Portal Apps integrate seamlessly with the Cobo Portal ecosystem, providing a unified experience for discovering, installing, and managing applications.

## Available apps

| App Name | Built By | Description                                              |
| ------------ | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| SuperLoop    | Cobo         | An off-exchange settlement network that allows institutions to trade on exchanges without worrying about counterparty risks. For more details, see [SuperLoop](/en/apps/superloop/introduction). |
| Staking      | Cobo         | An application that allows you to easily and securely grow your assets in wallets on Cobo Portal. For more details, see [Staking](/en/apps/staking/introduction). |
| Batch Payouts     | Cobo         | An application that is designed to streamline the process of making multiple transfers in one go.  |
