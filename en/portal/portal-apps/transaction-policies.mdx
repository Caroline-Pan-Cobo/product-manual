This article describes how to set up and update [transaction policies](/en/portal/risk-controls/tx-policies-overview) after the installation of Cobo Portal Apps.

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).

2. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the apps button"></img> in the main menu to open the **Apps** page.

3. In the **All Apps** section or the **Installed Apps** section, click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_advanced.svg" className="icon" alt="the settings icon"></img> on an app to open the **App Management** page.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/apps-overview.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Screenshot of Cobo Portal Apps" />

4. On the **App Management** page, switch to the **Transaction Policies** tab.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/app-settings.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The App Settings page" />

    <Info>If you install an app but have not set up transaction policies, there is a reminder message on both the <b>Apps</b> page and the app overview page. These reminders can redirect you to the <b>Transaction Policies</b> tab.</Info>

5. On the **Transaction Policies** tab, you can view the transaction policies applicable to the app. If a transaction policy is applicable to the app, it means the app is one of the conditions of the transaction policy, or the transaction policy is relevant to the wallets you allow the app to access.
    
    <Note>Different apps might have different default transaction policies.</Note>

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/transaction-policies.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The Transaction Policies page" />

    - To create a new transaction policy for the app, click **Create Policy** and choose **Token Transfer** to open the **Create Transfer Policy** page, or choose **Contract Call** to open the **Create Contract Call Policy** page. There you can create new transaction policies with conditions applied specifically to this app or any app as the transaction initiator. For how to set up transaction policies, see [Introduction to transaction policies](/en/portal/risk-controls/tx-policies-overview).
    - To adjust the priorities of the transaction policies, click **Adjust Priorities** to open the **Adjust Priorities** page.
    - To view the details of a transaction policy, click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_view.svg" className="icon" alt="the view icon"></img>.
    - To manage a transaction policy, click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_more.svg" className="icon" alt="the more icon"></img> in the **Operation** column and select the corresponding action such as **Transactions**, **Revisions**, **Edit**, and **Delete**.
