This article describes how to browse and install Cobo Portal Apps.

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/icon-apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the apps tab"></img> in the main menu on the left-hand side.

    The **All Apps** section displays all available Cobo Portal Apps.
    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/apps-overview.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Screenshot of Cobo Portal Apps" />
    

3. In the **All Apps** section, click an app to view its details, then, you can find the overview information and required permissions for the app.
4. Click **Install** to initiate the installation process. A dialog that lists required permissions appears.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/install.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Install button on the app" />

5. In the dialog, click **Confirm** to proceed with the installation.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/confirm-install.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Dialog of confirming installation" />

    **Note**: Depending on the [Risk Controls](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/portal/risk-controls/introduction) policies in your [organization](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/portal/organization/introduction), the installation might require approvals from your organization [admin](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/portal/organization/roles-and-permissions). When you click **Confirm**, your organization admin will receive an approval request for the installation.

6. You can hover over the **Installing** button to view the approval progress. Once the request is confirmed, the app will begin installing. 

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/installing.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The app is installing" />

7. Once the installation is completed, click **Open** to start using the app.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/open.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Open button on the app" />

    You can also navigate to the **Apps** page, where you can see your installed Cobo Portal App displayed in the **Installed Apps** section.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/installed.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The Installed Apps section" />
