## What is Fee Station?

Fee Station is your all-in-one solution for managing expenses. It automatically settles your Cobo bills and transaction fees for MPC Wallets. With Fee Station, you can enjoy the convenience of having your expenses deducted from a single, integrated account.

## Key features

### Simplified Payment Process
Fee Station automatically handles your Cobo bills, preventing overdue payments and eliminating the need to track outstanding bills.

### Automatic Payment of Transaction Fees

Fee Station can be configured to automatically cover transaction fees for your MPC Wallets and Asset Wallets (Web3 Wallets). This feature eliminates the need for ongoing balance monitoring and manual top-ups, ensuring that your transactions from these wallets are always covered.
