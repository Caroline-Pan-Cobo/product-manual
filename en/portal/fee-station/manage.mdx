Manage your Fee Station by clicking the corresponding icons:

- View Deposit Address <img src="/en/images/fee-station/address.svg" className="icon"></img>: See the deposit address for a specific token.
- Deposit <img src="/en/images/fee-station/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Deposit a specific token into your Fee Station. You can make the deposit by copying the tokenâ€™s deposit address or by scanning its QR code.
- View Transaction Records <img src="/en/images/fee-station/view.svg" className="icon"></img>: View detailed information about a Fee Station transaction, including the amount, timestamp, and more.
- Filter <img src="/en/images/fee-station/filter.svg" className="icon"></img>: Customize your view by filtering based on token type, balance amount, or token use (e.g., paying for Cobo bills).
- Sort <img src="/en/images/fee-station/sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Arrange your view by sorting tokens based on their balance amount, either from high to low or low to high.
- Search <img src="/en/images/fee-station/search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Quickly locate a token by searching for its name.
