---
title: "Deposit into Fee Station"
sidebarTitle: "Deposit into Fee Station"
---

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click your name in the bottom left corner, then select **Organization** > **Fee Station**.
3. Switch to the **Tokens** tab, where you will see a list of fee tokens supported by Fee Station.
4. Click <img src="/en/images/fee-station/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img> next to the token you want to deposit into Fee Station.
- Alternatively, click <img src="/en/images/fee-station/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img> on the top right corner and select the token you want to deposit into Fee Station.
<img src="/en/images/fee-station/2.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>
5. In the pop-up window, click <img src="/en/images/fee-station/copy.svg" className="icon"></img> to copy the deposit address or scan the QR code with your wallet.
- If you want to make a transfer from other wallets created on Cobo Portal, click **Deposit from other wallets**. Then, follow the instructions [here](/en/portal/transfers/introduction) to complete the transfer.
