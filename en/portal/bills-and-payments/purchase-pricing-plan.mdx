This topic introduces how to purchase a pricing plan. To learn which pricing plan you should choose, see [Pricing plans](/en/portal/bills-and-payments/introduction#pricing-plans).

## Purchase an Enterprise plan

[Reach out to our sales team](https://www.cobo.com/book-demo/?utm_source=product-manual&utm_medium=product&utm_campaign=pricing-plan-intro&utm_content=enterprise-contact-sales) if you want to purchase an Enterprise plan. For Custodial Wallets and Exchange Wallets, you will be required to go through Know Your Customer (KYC) verification before the purchase. The following are a few examples of documents and information you may be asked to provide during the process.

- Your company information, such as company name and registered address.
- Company documents, such as certified copies of the companyâ€™s certificate of incorporation and articles of association.
- Shareholder or Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) information.

## Purchase a Starter or Standard plan

Follow the steps below to purchase a Starter or Standard plan for MPC Wallets on Cobo Portal. 

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click the profile icon at the bottom left, and choose **Pricing Plans**. 
3. Select **MPC Wallets**.
<img src="/en/images/bills-and-payments/purchase-plan.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>
4. Click **Upgrade** or **Select Pricing Plan**, as shown on the screen.
5. On the **Select Pricing Plan** dialog, select either the **Wallet-as-a-Service** or **Treasury Management** tab, then click **Activate** under the pricing plan you want to purchase.
6. On the **Activate Pricing Plan** dialog, check the checkbox next to the service agreement, select a billing cycle, then click **Pay**.
7. On the **Select Payment Method** dialog, click **Select** for either **Fee Station** or **Cobo Pay**.
    - To pay with Fee Station, refer to [Introduction to Fee Station](/en/portal/fee-station/introduction)
    - To pay with Cobo Pay, select either **Pay with cryptos** or **Pay with fiat currencies**, then follow the on-screen instructions to complete your payment.
