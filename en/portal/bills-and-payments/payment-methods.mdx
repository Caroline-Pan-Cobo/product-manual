For both one-time bill payments and recurring payments for pricing plans, there are two payment methods:

- Fee Station, an all-in-one solution for managing expenses. To learn more about Fee Station, see [Introduction to Fee Station](/en/portal/fee-station/introduction).
- Cobo Pay, an easy and secure checkout experience using your preferred crypto wallet or bank card.

## Cobo Pay

Cobo Pay allows payment via cryptos and fiat currencies:

### Cryptos

Cobo Pay supports selected major networks and tokens. You can choose either of the following methods:

- Connect a wallet to pay directly
- Click **View Address** to display the wallet address and QR code


### Fiat currencies

Two methods are supported:

- **Bank transfer**: Transfer to Coboâ€™s designated account. Bank details are shown when this option is selected.  
  <Note>Include the invoice number in the transfer remarks to avoid delays and manual review.</Note>  
  After completing the transfer, click **Next Step** and confirm your transfer details.  
  <Note>The payment will only be considered complete once the funds are actually received by Cobo.</Note>

- **Card payment**: Enter card information to complete the payment. A 5% processing fee applies.
