## July 17, 2024

This release brings several new features:

- [Smart Contract Wallets](/en/portal/smart-contract-wallets/introduction): This wallet type supports a myriad of smart contract wallets, prominently featuring Safe{Wallet} alongside other account abstraction wallets. It also allows the flexibility to delegate an Externally Owned Accounts (EOA) wallet to interact seamlessly with the smart contract ecosystem.
- [Exchange Wallets](/en/portal/exchange-wallets/introduction): Exchange Wallets are your one-stop solution for managing multiple exchange accounts effortlessly. It brings all of your exchange accounts together in a single, user-friendly interface, providing a seamless experience for viewing, monitoring, and managing your assets across various exchanges.
- [Cobo Portal Apps](/en/portal/portal-apps/introduction): Leveraging the advanced capabilities of the WaaS 2.0 API, Cobo collaborates with diverse third-party providers to create a robust ecosystem of business applications. These applications, known as Cobo Portal Apps, are designed to enhance digital asset management and accessibility.
- [SuperLoop](/en/portal/portal-apps/superloop/introduction): As the first Cobo Portal Apps developed by Cobo, SuperLoop is an off-exchange settlement network that allows institutions to trade on exchanges without worrying about counterparty risks.

- [Pricing plans](/en/portal/bills-and-payments/introduction): You can select from various pricing plans tailored to different business requirements, including free trials. 
- Fee Station: You can now make payments for your pricing plans, add-on usages, and bills with Fee Station, a wallet specifically designed for fee payments.

## June 10, 2024

This is the first release of [Cobo Portal](/en/portal/introduction), an all-in-one platform designed to provide a secure and efficient way to manage your digital assets. With this release, Cobo Portal offers the following features:

- [Custodial Wallets](/en/portal/custodial-wallets/introduction) and [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/introduction) combined in one platform.
- [Risk Controls](/en/portal/risk-controls/introduction), which offer comprehensive risk control mechanisms designed to address the challenges in storing, managing, and transferring digital assets, including off-chain transaction policies, governance policies, and user roles and permissions.

- [Developer Console](/en/portal/developer-console/introduction), which is a central hub for developers to manage API keys, track API logs, oversee webhook events, and handle callback messages.
- [Dashboard](/en/portal/dashboard/introduction), which provides a comprehensive overview of your total asset value, asset allocations, transactions, pricing plan usage, and the latest Cobo ecosystem updates.

To learn more about Cobo Portal, see [Introduction to Cobo Portal](/en/portal/introduction).
