#### Content additions and updates for Cobo Portal features


<Steps>
    <Step title="August 9, 2024" icon="star">
    - [Staking](/en/apps/staking/introduction) *(Coming soon on [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login))*: A [Cobo Portal App](/en/portal/portal-apps/introduction) developed by Cobo that enables staking with protocols such as the [Babylon protocol](https://babylonlabs.io/) directly within your wallets in Cobo Portal.
    - [Cobo Connect](/en/cobo-connect/introduction) *(Coming soon on [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login))*: A [Chrome extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/category/extensions) that enables you to connect your Cobo Portal wallets with dApps. After you make the connection, Cobo Connect will identify each transaction and message from the dApps and process them based on your pre-configured [risk control policies](/en/portal/risk-controls/introduction) in Cobo Portal.
    - [Fee Station](/en/portal/fee-station/introduction): Fee Station is your all-in-one solution for managing expenses. It automatically settles your Cobo bills, service fees, and transaction fees for [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/introduction) from a single, integrated account.
    - [Cobo Portal Apps](/en/portal/portal-apps/introduction): For each app, you now can manage its optional permissions, wallet scopes, and transaction policies.
    - [Supported token and chains](/en/portal/supported-tokens-custodial): You can now check Cobo's supported tokens and chains for [Custodial Wallets](/en/portal/supported-tokens-custodial) and [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/supported-tokens-mpc) on the Product Manuals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="July 17, 2024" icon="star">
    - [Smart Contract Wallets](/en/portal/smart-contract-wallets/introduction): This wallet type supports a myriad of smart contract wallets, prominently featuring Safe\{Wallet\} alongside other account abstraction wallets. It also allows the flexibility to delegate an Externally Owned Accounts (EOA) wallet to interact seamlessly with the smart contract ecosystem.
    - [Exchange Wallets](/en/portal/exchange-wallets/introduction): Exchange Wallets are your one-stop solution for managing multiple exchange accounts effortlessly. It brings all of your exchange accounts together in a single, user-friendly interface, providing a seamless experience for viewing, monitoring, and managing your assets across various exchanges.
    - [Cobo Portal Apps](/en/portal/portal-apps/introduction): Leveraging the capabilities of the WaaS 2.0 API, Cobo partners with a diverse range of third-party providers to establish a robust business application ecosystem known as Cobo Portal Apps, which are a collection of advanced tools for investing, trading, and efficiently managing digital assets.
    - [SuperLoop](/en/apps/superloop/introduction): As the first Cobo Portal App developed by Cobo, SuperLoop is an off-exchange settlement network that allows institutions to trade on exchanges without worrying about counterparty risks.
    - [Pricing plans](/en/portal/bills-and-payments/introduction): You can select from various pricing plans tailored to different business requirements, including free trials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="June 10, 2024" icon="star">
    This is the first release of [Cobo Portal](/en/portal/introduction), an all-in-one platform designed to provide a secure and efficient way to manage your digital assets. With this release, Cobo Portal offers the following features:

    - [Custodial Wallets](/en/portal/custodial-wallets/introduction) and [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/introduction) combined in one platform.
    - [Risk Controls](/en/portal/risk-controls/introduction), which offer comprehensive risk control mechanisms designed to address the challenges in storing, managing, and transferring digital assets, including off-chain transaction policies, governance policies, and user roles and permissions.
    - [Developer Console](/en/portal/developer-console/introduction), which is a central hub for developers to manage API keys, track API logs, oversee webhook events, and handle callback messages.
    - [Dashboard](/en/portal/dashboard/introduction), which provides a comprehensive overview of your total asset value, asset allocations, transactions, pricing plan usage, and the latest Cobo ecosystem updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
