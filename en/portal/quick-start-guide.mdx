## Overview

This quick start guide walks you through the entire journey, from being a visitor at Cobo to becoming a free trial user and eventually a paid customer.

## General setups
<Steps>
  <Step icon="1" title="Start free trial, create a Cobo Account, and create an organization">
    <iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/gNWGhrKWcgY?si=kcazFmI0QPuWT253" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>
    <Note>If a Cobo sales representative has sent you an email invitation, you can skip this section. Simply follow the instructions in the email, then continue from the **Set up Cobo Guard** section in **General setups**.</Note>
    <Note>Your [free trial](/en/portal/bills-and-payments/introduction#pricing-plans) will start as soon as you complete this section.</Note>
    1. Go to [cobo.com](https://www.cobo.com/), then click **Start trial**.
    2. Enter your **Email**, **Name**, and **Company Name**, then click **Sign up**.
    4. On the **Sign Up** page, confirm your **Email**, then click **Continue**.
    5. On the **Password** page, enter your **Password** and **Confirm Password**, then click **Continue**.
    6. On the **Email Verification** page, enter the verification code sent to your email address, then click **Continue**.
    7. On the **Google Authenticator** page, follow the on screen instructions to download and install Google Authenticator, scan the QR code with Google Authenticator, and enter the Google Authenticator code, then click **Submit**.
    7. On the **Almost There!** page, enter an organization name, a **Promo Code (Optional)**, then click **Get Started**.
    9. On the **Congratulations!** Page, review the information and check the checkboxes, then click **Confirm**.
    {/* 9.  *(Coming soon)* This video shows step 1 to 8 in action: <iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/cDsaEapQ1_E?si=uovqU34qpxMMXSvW" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>*/}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up Cobo Guard">
    {/* 1. On the [**Welcome to Cobo Portal**](https://portal.cobo.com/guide) page, click **Go** next to **Set Up Cobo Guard** to open the **Log In** page of Cobo Accounts. */}
    <iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ykPiHV8_gik?si=WrEMi4PhXE_6BQpK" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>
    1. On the [**Welcome to Cobo Portal**](https://portal.cobo.com/guide) page, click **Go** next to **Set Up Cobo Guard** to open the **Log In** page of [Cobo Accounts](/en/accounts/introduction).
    2. Enter the **Email** and **Password** you used to sign up for the free trial, then click **Log In**.
    3. On the Cobo Accounts page, click **MFA Management**, then click **Set up** next to **Cobo Guard**.
    4. Follow the on-screen instructions and download [Cobo Guard](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/guard/introduction), then click **Continue**.
    5. On the **Set Up Cobo Guard** page, tap <img src="/en/images/cobo-guard/common/icon_scan.svg" className="icon"></img> on your Cobo Guard app, then scan the QR code with your Cobo Guard.
    6. On your Cobo Guard, you will see a **Link Cobo Accounts** page. Click **Link**.
    7. You have now successfully linked Cobo Guard. On your computer, click **Continue** on the **Successful** page to go back to the Cobo Accounts page.
    {/* 7. *(Coming soon)* This video shows step 1 to 6 in action: <iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/cDsaEapQ1_E?si=uovqU34qpxMMXSvW" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>*/}
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Specific setups

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For treasury management users">
    ### Setups for treasury management users
    <Note>This section is for treasury management users who will primarily work on the Cobo Portal to create wallets and make transactions. If you are a developer interested in creating wallets and making transactions using Cobo's [Wallet-as-a-Service (WaaS) 2.0 API](https://www.cobo.com/developers/v2/guides/overview/introduction), it is recommended that you still go through this section before proceeding to the **For developers using the WaaS API** section.</Note>
    <Info>Cobo Portal provides four types of wallets: [Custodial Wallets](/en/portal/custodial-wallets/introduction), [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/introduction), [Smart Contract Wallets](/en/portal/smart-contract-wallets/introduction), and [Exchange Wallets](/en/portal/exchange-wallets/introduction). In this section, you'll learn how to create an [MPC Wallet (Organization-Controlled Wallet)](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/introduction). If you prefer to create a different type of wallet, refer to the **Create other types of wallets and make transactions** section under **Setups for treasury management users** for detailed instructions.</Info>
  <Steps>
      <Step title="Create an MPC Wallet (Organization-Controlled Wallet)">
        <iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/b6EZO9MU7VU?si=mfefe-WwAcUdgGYY" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>
        <Info>Creating an MPC Wallet consists of 3 essential steps: Creating a vault, a Main Group, and a wallet.</Info>
        1. Go to the [Welcome to Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/guide) page, then click **Go** next to **Create an MPC Wallet**.
        2. Click **Create Vault** to create a vault. For more information, see [Create an MPC vault](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/create-mpc-vault).
        3. Click **Create Main Group** to create a Main Group. For more information, see [Create a Main Group](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/holder-group-main-group) and follow the **Mobile Co-Signer** route.
        4. Create a wallet. For more information, see [Create an MPC Wallet](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/create-mpc-wallet).
        <Info>After completing step 3, a default wallet would have been automatically created.</Info>
        {/* 5. *(Coming soon)* This video shows step 1 to 4 in action: <iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/cDsaEapQ1_E?si=uovqU34qpxMMXSvW" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>*/}

        <iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/twwLXic_bLM?si=fL2lVrJbcYWBjDxS" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>
        1. Now that your MPC Wallet is ready, you can start making transactions. To make a deposit, see [Deposit from external wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/transfers).
        <Info>There's no need to set up transaction policies as instructed in [Deposit from external wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/transfers) at this stage when you are just trying out the feature. When you are ready to scale, ensure to set up appropriate transaction policies.</Info>
        2. To make a withdrawal, see [Transfer from MPC Wallets (Organization-Controlled Wallets)](/en/portal/transfers/from-mpc-wallets).
