Track the total asset value of your portfolio in USD, BTC, or ETH across various time periods (**Daily**, **Weekly**, **Monthly**, or **Yearly**).

<img src="/en/images/dashboard/total-assets-trend.svg" className="screenshot_modal"/>

<Note>The asset value is captured daily at 23:59:59 UTC+8.</Note>
<Note>No data will be shown if your account has no recorded transactions or deposits. Please add funds to start tracking transactions and account activities.</Note>
