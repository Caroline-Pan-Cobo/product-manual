Cobo Portal’s Dashboard is your central hub for monitoring and managing your digital assets. It provides a comprehensive overview of your total asset value, details of your subscription package usage, and the latest Cobo ecosystem updates. With easy-to-understand bar graphs and pie charts, Dashboard visually breaks down your asset allocations and transactions to provide a clear and concise snapshot of your portfolio and operations.
