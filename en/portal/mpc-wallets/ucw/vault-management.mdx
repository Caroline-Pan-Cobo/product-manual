## Overview

A project is a collection of vaults, and a vault is a collection of wallets. See [Object model](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ucw/object-model).

## Create a vault
A vault can only be created using the [WaaS 2.0 API](https://www.cobo.com/developers/v2/guides/overview/introduction). To create a vault, see **Complete the Initial setup** > **Create a vault** on [Get started with MPC Wallets (User-Controlled Wallets)](https://www.cobo.com/developers/v2/guides/mpc-wallets/get-started-ucw#1-complete-the-initial-setup).

## Manage a vault

Once you've [created a project](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ucw/project-management#create-a-project) and [created a vault](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ucw/vault-management#create-a-vault), you can manage your vault.

Follow these instructions to manage a specific vault.

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/common/wallets_icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets** > **User-Controlled Wallets**, then click a project to open its own management page, then click a vault to enter its management page.

On the vault's management page, you can manage both the vault and the wallets in it. You can click **Export Addresses & Balances** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_export_balances_and_addresses.svg" className="icon"></img>, **Transactions** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>, **Key Share Management** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_key_share_management.svg" className="icon"></img>, or **Edit Vault Name** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img> to manage the vault.

To manage the wallets in the vault, find a wallet you want to manage, then click **View Transaction History** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>, **Edit Wallet Name** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>, or **View Details** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_view.svg" className="icon"></img>.
