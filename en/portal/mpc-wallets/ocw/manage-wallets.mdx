Follow the steps below to navigate to your wallet:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets**.
3. Select your vault.
4. Select your wallet.

Manage your wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons.

<img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/manage-wallet-token.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

## Wallet

- **Token Management** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/token-management.svg" className="icon"></img>: Activate and display new tokens on your wallet.
- **View Transactions** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View your wallet activity, including a detailed history of past and pending transactions.
- **Edit Address Label** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Edit wallet name.

## Tokens
- **Deposit** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Initiate a deposit into your wallet address.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_withdraw.svg" className="icon"></img>: Make a withdrawal from your wallet address.
- **View Deposit Addresses** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit address.svg" className="icon"></img>: View deposit addresses of the corresponding token.
- **View Transactions** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View the transaction history of the corresponding token.
- **Search** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Quickly locate your wallet by searching with the wallet name, wallet address, and label.
- **Filter** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_filter.svg" className="icon"></img>: Tailor your view by filtering addresses based on network, asset type, last activity time, balance amount.
- **Sort** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Organize your addresses by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.

## Addresses

- **Create Address** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/add-address.svg" className="icon"></img>: Generate a new unique receiving wallet address.
- **View Transactions** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View the transaction history of the corresponding address.
- **Deposit** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Initiate a deposit into your wallet address.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_withdraw.svg" className="icon"></img>: Make a withdrawal from your wallet address.
- **Edit Address Label** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Modify the label that is tagged to your address.
- **Search** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Quickly locate your wallet by searching with the wallet name, wallet address, and label.
- **Filter**: Tailor your view by filtering addresses based on network, asset type, last activity time, balance amount.
- **Sort** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Organize your addresses by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.
