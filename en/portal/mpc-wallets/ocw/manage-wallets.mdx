Follow the steps below to navigate to your wallet:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets**.
3. Select your vault.
4. Select your wallet.

Manage your wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons.

<img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/manage-wallet-token.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

## Wallet

- **View Transactions** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View the transaction history of the wallet.
- **Edit Wallet Name** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Edit the wallet name.

## Tokens
- **Search** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Search by token name.
- **Filter** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_filter.svg" className="icon"></img>: Filter by token, last activity time, and balance.
- **Sort** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Sort by last activity time or balance.
- **Add Tokens**: Add token types to your wallet.

In the row for each token:
- **Deposit** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Initiate a deposit of the token into the wallet.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_withdraw.svg" className="icon"></img>: Make a withdrawal of the token from the wallet.
- **View Deposit Addresses** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit address.svg" className="icon"></img>: View deposit addresses of the token.
- **View Transactions** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View the transaction history related to the token in the wallet.

## Addresses

Click **Addresses** to switch to the **Addresses** tab.

<img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/manage-address.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

- **Search** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Search by address or label.
- **Filter**: Filter addresses by network.
- **Sort** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Sort your addresses by created time.
- **Add Address**: Generate a new unique wallet address.

In the row for each address:
- **View Transactions** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View the transaction history of the address.
- **Deposit** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Initiate a deposit into the address.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_withdraw.svg" className="icon"></img>: Make a withdrawal from the address.
- **Edit Address Label** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Modify the address labels.
