## Deposit from external wallets

Follow the steps below to deposit from external wallets:
1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets**.
3. Select your vault.
4. Select your wallet.
5. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/token-management.svg" className="icon"></img> to open the **Token Management** tab. Enter a **Token** such as **BTC**, then in the search results, click **Apply for Activation** under **Network** to open the **Chain Management** page.
6. On the **Chain Management** page, click **Add Chains**, select the chains you want to add, click **Confirm**, then complete a security MFA confirmation.
7. Close the **Token Management** page to return to your wallet.
8. Click **Addresses**, then click **Add Address** to open the **Create Address** page. Select a chain and needed address types, then click **Create** to create a deposit address.
9. Under the **Address** tab or **Tokens** tab, navigate to the address or token you want to deposit into, and then click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>.
<img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/deposit.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>
10. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
11. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you want to deposit into.
