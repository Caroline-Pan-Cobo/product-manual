The Custodial Wallets' dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of consolidated balances in each of your wallets. For more detailed insights, navigate to the respective wallet to view the breakdown of balances by asset type as well as the associated deposit addresses.

Manage your Asset Wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons.

<img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/manage.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

## Wallet

- **Deposit** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/deposit-icon.png" className="icon"/>: Initiate a deposit into the wallet.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/withdraw-icon.png" className="icon"/>: Make a withdrawal from the wallet.
- **View Transaction History** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/transaction-history.png" className="icon"/>: View the transaction history of the wallet.

## Tokens
- **Search** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/search.svg" className="icon"/>: Search by token name.
- **Filter** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/filter.png" className="icon"/>: Filter by network, token, and balance.
- **Sort** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/sort.png" className="icon"/>: Sort by trading time or balance.
- **Add Tokens**: Add token types to your wallet.

In the row for each token:
- **Deposit** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/deposit-icon.png" className="icon"/>: Initiate a deposit of the token into the wallet.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/withdraw-icon.png" className="icon"/>: Make a withdrawal of the token from the wallet.
- **View Deposit Addresses** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/view-deposit-address.png" className="icon"/>: View deposit addresses of the token.
- **View Transaction History** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/transaction-history.png" className="icon"/>: View the transaction history related to the token in the wallet.
