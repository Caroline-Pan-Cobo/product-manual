## Institutional-grade wallet security
Custodial Wallets utilize advanced encryption techniques and a battle-tested risk control engine to ensure that your funds are protected against unauthorized access and potential attacks. They implement a robust 3-tier (hot-warm-cold) private key storage architecture with 95% of funds securely stored in cold wallets and only 5% in hot and warm wallets.

## Customizable access controls and risk policies
Custodial Wallets offer role-based access controls and customizable risk policies to tailor to your specific needs. You can set up roles such as Admin, Approver, Spender, Operator, and Viewer to separate user privileges. You can also configure and automate authorization workflows to streamline your operations. To ensure the integrity of each operation, Cobo Guard serves as a multi-factor authentication (MFA) to support multi-role and multi-level authentication. To learn more about Cobo Guard, see [Introduction to Cobo Guard](/en/guard/introduction).

## Automate token sweep from all your end users
Custodial Wallets' token sweep function lets you automatically consolidate tokens from all your end users’ deposit addresses while saving on gas fees.

## Industry widest token and chain support
Custodial Wallets offer the industry’s most comprehensive support of tokens and blockchain networks. Combined with its swift integration of new tokens, Custodial Wallets ensure that you can stay ahead of the competition and maximize your engagement with the crypto economy and the ever-expanding universe of digital assets.

## User-friendly interface
Custodial Wallets' intuitive and user-friendly interface allows both novice and experienced users to navigate and execute transactions securely and effortlessly. Transfer assets, manage and track portfolios, engage with external applications, set up real-time alerts, and more, with our streamlined interface for a seamless experience.
