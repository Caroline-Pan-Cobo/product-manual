## Create an Asset Wallet

Asset Wallets are a subtype of Custodial Wallets, which allow you to transfer tokens (initiate deposits or withdrawals). You may create multiple wallets according to your business needs. To set up an Asset Wallet on Cobo Portal, follow these simple steps:

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/wallet-icon.svg" className="icon"/> and select **Custodial Wallets**.

<img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/custodial-wallet-dashboard.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>
3. Click **Create Wallet**.
4. Fill in your preferred **Wallet Name** (up to 30 characters), select **Asset Wallet** as **Wallet Type**, then click **Create**.
5. Once successful, you will see the newly created wallet on your dashboard.
