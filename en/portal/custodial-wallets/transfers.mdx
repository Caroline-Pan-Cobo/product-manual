<Note>Before making transfers using your Custodial Wallets, ensure that transaction policies have been set up in your organization. To learn more about transaction policies, see <a href="/en/portal/risk-controls/tx-policies-overview">Introduction to transaction policies</a>.</Note>

## Deposit from external wallets

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Custodial Wallets**.
3. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_view.svg" className="icon"></img> next to the wallet you want to deposit into.
4. Locate the type of tokens you want to deposit into the wallet, and then click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>.
5. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
6. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you have copied.

## Deposit from another wallet created on Cobo Portal

To learn how to deposit into your Custodial Wallets from another wallet created on Cobo Portal, see [Introduction to Transfer](/en/portal/transfers/introduction).
