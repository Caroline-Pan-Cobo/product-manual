## Deposit from external wallets

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Custodial Wallets**.
3. Click the **View Details** icon next to the wallet you want to deposit into.
4. Locate the type of tokens you want to deposit into the wallet, and then click <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>.
5. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
6. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you have copied.
