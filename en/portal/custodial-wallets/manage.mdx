The Custodial Wallets' dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of consolidated balances in each of your wallets. For more detailed insights, navigate to the respective wallet to view the breakdown of balances by asset type as well as the associated deposit addresses.

Manage your Custodial Wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons:

- **Deposit** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/deposit-icon.png" className="icon"/>: Initiate a deposit into the selected wallet address.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/withdraw-icon.png" className="icon"/>: Make a withdrawal from the selected wallet address.
- **View Deposit Addresses** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/view-deposit-address.png" className="icon"/>: View the deposit wallet addresses associated with the selected asset type.
- **Transaction History** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/transaction-history.png" className="icon"/>: View your wallet activity, including a detailed transaction history of past and pending transactions.
- **Filter** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/filter.png" className="icon"/>: Tailor your view by filtering wallets based on blockchain network, asset type, or balance amount.
- **Sort** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/sort.png" className="icon"/>: Organize your accounts by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.
- **Token Management** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_token_management.svg" className="icon"/>: Search for tokens you want to add to the wallet.
- **Search** <img src="/en/images/custodial-wallet/search.svg" className="icon"/>: Quickly search by token name.
