---
title: "Unsupported tokens and chains"
sidebarTitle: "Unsupported tokens and chains"
---

All tokens and chains included in your subscription package with Cobo's Custodial Wallets will be visible in the wallets' interface. Should you need support for a token or chain that is not included in your subscription package or not currently supported by Cobo, please contact Cobo's customer support at [support@cobo.com](mailto:support@cobo.com) to request assistance.
