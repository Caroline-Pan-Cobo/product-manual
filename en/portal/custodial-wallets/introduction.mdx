Cobo Portal's Custodial Wallets are a secure and regulated custodial wallet solution, designed specifically for institutions to manage digital assets. Custodial Wallets take the complexity out of securing your assets by managing the private keys on your behalf. ## Types of Custodial Wallets on Cobo Portal

Custodial Wallets on Cobo Portal are divided into two types:
- Asset Wallets: Fund custodial wallets primarily for token transfers, with centralized fund management.
- Web3 Wallets: Address custodial wallets that support individual addresses. In addition to token transfers, Web3 Wallets support on-chain operations like contract calls and message signing.
