You can manage an on-chain transaction policy by navigating to Smart Contract Wallets > On-Chain Transaction Policies. The Current tab displays all active on-chain transaction policies, the Queue tab shows on-chain transaction policies awaiting batch submission for multi-sig confirmations by Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signers, and the History tab lists all submitted multi-sig transaction requests.

**Under the Current tab**:
- View Policy Details: Check policy details, including the policy name, status, and If as well as Then conditions.

**Under the Queue tab**:
- View: Check policy details, including the policy name, status, and If as well as Then conditions.
- Revoke: Cancel a request that is currently pending batch submission for multi-sig confirmations by Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signers.

**Under the History tab**:
- View: Check the details of a submitted multi-sig transaction request, including the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; transaction hash, nonce, Delegate addresses, and If as well as Then conditions.
