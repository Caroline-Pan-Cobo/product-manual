User roles consist of predefined sets of rules that allow you to assign specific permissions to designated members within your [Organization](/en/portal/organization/introduction). Cobo offers five preset roles, and you can also create additional roles tailored to your specific requirements. To learn more about user roles and permissions, see [Introduction to user roles and permissions](/en/portal/organization/roles-and-permissions).
