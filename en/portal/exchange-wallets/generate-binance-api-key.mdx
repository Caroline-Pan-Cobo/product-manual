Generate the **API Key** and **API Secret Key** for your Binance account by following these steps:

1) Log in to your Binance account.
2) Navigate to **Account** > **API management**.
3) Click **Create API**.
4) Select **System generated**, and click **Next**.
<img src="/en/images/exchange-wallet/3.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>
5) Tick the following **4 API restrictions**:<br />
    a) Enable Reading<br />
    b) Enable Internal Transfer<br />
    c) Permit Universal Transfer<br />
    d) Enable Withdrawal
