The Exchange Wallets dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of consolidated balances on each exchange. For more detailed insights, navigate to the respective exchange to view the breakdown of balances by account. Additionally, a breakdown by asset type within each account is available by clicking into the respective accounts.

Manage the linked accounts in your Exchange Wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons:

- **Deposit** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/10.png" className="icon"></img>: Initiate a deposit into the account. Deposits can be done on Main Account only.
- **Withdraw** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/11.png" className="icon"></img>: Make a withdrawal from the account. Only certain exchange account types allow funds to be withdrawn from the Main Accounts to an external address. Refer to the [**Transfer from Exchange Wallets**](/en/portal/transfers/from-exchange-wallets) section for more information.
- **Transfer In** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/12.png" className="icon"></img> / **Transfer Out** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/13.png" className="icon"></img>: Transfer assets between your Main Account and Sub Account, or between different account types (e.g. between Funding Account and Trading Account).
- **View Transactions** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/14.png" className="icon"></img>: View your wallet activity, including a detailed history of past and pending transactions.
- **API Management** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/15.png" className="icon"></img>: Navigate to the respective exchange to generate your API key and API secret key.
- **Refresh** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/16.png" className="icon"></img>: Update and synchronize your asset balances with the latest data for your linked account(s).
- **Unlink Account** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/17.png" className="icon"></img>: Disconnect your linked account(s).
- **Link Sub Account** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/18.png" className="icon"></img>: Add your exchange sub account(s) to the associated main account.
- **Filter** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/19.png" className="icon"></img>: Tailor your view by filtering wallets based on blockchain network, asset type, last activity time, or balance amount.
- **Sort** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/20.png" className="icon"></img>: Organize your accounts by sorting based on last activity time or balance amount.
- **Search** <img src="/images/exchange-wallet/21.png" className="icon"></img>: Quickly locate your selected exchange account or token holding, by searching with the account name or asset name.
