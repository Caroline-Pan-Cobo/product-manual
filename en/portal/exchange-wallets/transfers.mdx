## Deposit from external wallets

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Exchange Wallets**.
3. Navigate to and click on the account you want to deposit into.
4. Locate the type of tokens you want to deposit into the account, and click the **View Deposit Addresses** icon.
5. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
6. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you have copied.
