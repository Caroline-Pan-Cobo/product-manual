## Deposit from external wallets

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Exchange Wallets**.
3. Navigate to the account you want to deposit into, and then click <img src="/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>.
4. Select the token type for the transfer. Selection is limited to tokens included in your organizationâ€™s subscription package. To enable a new token type (fees apply), click Not enabled.
5. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
6. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you wish to deposit into.
