This article describes how to transfer assets to your Exchange Wallets.

## Transfer within an Exchange Wallet

Transfer within an Exchange Wallet refers to the following cases:
- From the Main Account to its Sub Accounts
- From a Sub Account to its associated Main Account
- From a Sub Account to another Sub Account of its associated Main Account

For these cases, refer to [Transfer from Exchange Wallets](/en/portal/transfers/from-exchange-wallets) for instructions.

## Deposit into an Exchange Wallet
Deposits into an Exchange Wallet means that the assets are transferred from other Cobo wallet than an Exchange Wallet or en external address. You can only deposit into an Exchange Wallet (Main Account). If you want to add balance to a Exchange Wallet (Sub Account), you need first deposit into it associated Main Account, and then transfer the assets from the Main Account to the Sub Account. 

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Exchange Wallets**.
3. Navigate to and click the Exchange Wallet (Main) that you want to deposit into. 
    <img src="/en/images/exchange-wallet/exchange new 4.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>
    
4. Click the Depotis icon or the View Deposit Addresses icon.

    <img src="/en/images/exchange-wallet/exchange new 5.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

5. Copy the deposit address. 
6. Proceed to your external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address that you have copied.

    <Info>If you deposit into your Exchange Wallets from another Cobo wallet created on Cobo Portal, refer to <a href="/en/portal/exchange-wallets/transfers">Transfer</a> for instructions for different wallet types.</Info>
