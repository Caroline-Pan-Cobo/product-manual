## What are Exchange Wallets?
Cobo Portal's Exchange Wallets are your one-stop solution for managing multiple exchange accounts effortlessly.

It brings all of your exchange accounts together in a single, user-friendly interface, providing a seamless experience for viewing, monitoring, and managing your assets across various exchanges.

Exchange Wallets make it easy for you to transfer your assets between your exchange accounts and your wallets in just a few clicks - all without the hassle of logging in and out, and navigating through different exchange platforms.

## Key features

### Move assets in and out of multiple exchanges from a single dashboard
Exchange Wallets enable users to effortlessly transfer assets in and out of their exchange accounts. View, manage, and monitor your assets across different exchanges within a single dashboard, ensuring efficiency with a consolidated portfolio view.

### Customize security settings across multiple exchange accounts
Ensuring security across multiple exchange accounts can be a daunting task. Exchange Wallets simplify this by enabling users to customize access controls and risk policies across your exchange accounts directly from one unified platform. Establish user roles, set withdrawal limits, whitelist addresses, and benefit from enhanced security measures designed to safeguard your digital assets.

### Consolidated view of all your exchange accounts for enhanced portfolio oversight
In addition to providing a consolidated view of the entire portfolio across various exchanges, Exchange Wallets offer real-time monitoring capabilities. Receive instant notifications for deposits, withdrawals, and other updates, and make informed decisions on your asset allocation and risk management.
