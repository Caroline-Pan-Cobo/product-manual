Manage your Smart Contract Wallets easily by clicking the corresponding icons:

- Deposit <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Transfer funds into your wallet.
- Withdraw <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_withdraw.svg" className="icon"></img>: Move funds from your wallet to another wallet created on Cobo Portal or an EOA address.
- Edit Wallet Name <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Edit the name of your wallet.
- Copy Wallet Address <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_copy.svg" className="icon"></img>: Copy the wallet address of any wallet.
- View Wallet Details <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_view.svg" className="icon"></img>: View details such as your Cobo Safe address, Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; address, asset types, balances, and more.
- View Transaction History <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_view tx.svg" className="icon"></img>: View your walletâ€™s activity with a detailed transaction history, including past and pending transactions.
- Settings <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_settings.svg" className="icon"></img>: Modify wallet name, label, Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signer addresses, and signing threshold.
- Filter <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_filter.svg" className="icon"></img>: Filter the wallets so that only wallets that meet your specified criteria are displayed.
- Sort <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Sort your wallets so that they are displayed in the order based on the chosen criteria.
- Search <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Search for specific wallets using their names, addresses, or labels.
- Change Views <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_grid.svg" className="icon"></img>: Click the corresponding buttons to switch between grid view and list view when viewing your wallets.
