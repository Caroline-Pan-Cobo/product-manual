1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click **Wallets** > **Smart Contract Wallets** > <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_green.svg" className="icon"></img> or <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_grey.svg" className="icon"></img> next to the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; you want to use.
3. In the pop-up window, Click **Manage Delegate Key**.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/6.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>
4. To add more keys, click **+ Add Delegate Key**. To remove any Delegate Key that is no longer in use, click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_delete.svg" className="icon"></img>.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/7.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>
