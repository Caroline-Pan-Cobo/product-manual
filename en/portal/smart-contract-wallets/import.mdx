sidebarTitle: "Import a Smart Contract Wallet"
---

Note: Cobo Portal currently does not support deleting Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; once it has been imported.

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_wallet.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Smart Contract Wallets** > <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_import.svg" className="icon"></img>.
3. In the pop-up window, select Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; and click Next.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/2.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>
4. We will load all Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; instances with Cobo Safe enabled in Argus that are connected to your current email address.
- If you do not have any Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; in Argus with Cobo Safe enabled, please follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup on Argus.
- Alternatively, you can close the current window and click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_create.svg" className="icon"></img> in the upper-right corner to create a Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; directly on Cobo Portal.
5. Select the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; instances you want to import and click **Import**.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/3.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>
6. Your Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; instances are now successfully imported from Argus into Cobo Portal.
