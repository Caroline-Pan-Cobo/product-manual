---
title: "Add Delegates to Smart Contract Wallets"
sidebarTitle: "Add Delegates to Smart Contract Wallets"
---
1. Click into a Smart Contract Wallet and switch to the **Delegates** tab.
2. Click **Add a Delegate**.
3. Select an address from your MPC Wallets (Organization-Controlled Wallets) to designate as your Delegate.
4. (Optional) If you need a new address, click **Add New Delegate**, select a wallet type to continue, and click **Create Wallet**.
