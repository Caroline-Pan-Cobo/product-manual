---
title: "Top up gas fees"
sidebarTitle: "Top up gas fees for Delegates"
lang: "en"
Description: "Understand how to top up gas fees for Smart Contract Wallets in Cobo Portal, ensuring smooth transaction processing."
---

1. Switch to the **Delegates** tab.
2. Locate the **Balance** column, and click the **gas icon** next to the Delegate you added.
3. Copy the deposit address to top up gas fees.
4. (Optional) If you wish to deposit from any of your wallets created on Cobo Portal, click **Deposit from other wallets**.
