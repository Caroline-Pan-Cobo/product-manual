## Delete or add Delegates

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click **Wallets** > **Smart Contract Wallets** > **Current**.
3. Click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_edit.svg" className="icon"></img> next to the policy for which you want to edit the associated Delegates.
4. Add or remove Delegates addresses based on your requirements.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/10.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>
5. Click **Next** and review your policy details.
6. A multi-sig confirmation from Safe{Wallet} signers is required. Click **Submit** to submit a multi-sig transaction request or **Add to Queue** to submit your multi-sig transaction request to the queue.

## Top up gas fees for Delegates

1. If your Delegate is an EOA address, please deposit the required fee tokens directly into that address. This process is similar to topping up gas fees for your Delegates.
2. If your Delegate is an address from your MPC Wallet (Organization-Controlled), follow the steps below to top up fees:
- Navigate to **Smart Contract Wallets** > **On-Chain Transaction Policies**.
- Under Delegates, click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_copy.svg" className="icon"></img> to copy a Delegate address associated with your selected policy.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/11.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"></img>
- Navigate back to **MPC Wallets** > **Organization-Controlled** > **Addresses**.
- Paste the copied address in the search bar.
- Locate your Delegate address and click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img> to top up gas fees.
