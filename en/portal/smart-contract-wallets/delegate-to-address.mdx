---
title: "Add Delegates to my addresses on Argus"
sidebarTitle: "Add Delegates to my addresses"
---

1. On Cobo Portal, click **Need Authorization** next to the Delegate you just added.
2. In the pop-up window, click **Authorize**. You will be redirected to the Cobo Argus platform. Do make sure that you are using the same Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; as the one you imported into Cobo Portal.
3. You can add Delegates to **My Addresses** on Cobo Argus using one of the following methods:
    - Method 1: Click **Add Address** when authorizing your Delegates with the on-chain transfer role.  For more information, please refer to [Authorize Delegates](/en/portal/smart-contract-wallets/authorize-delegate).
    - Method 2: Head to **Me** > **My Addresses** to add your Delegates:
        - On Cobo Argus, click **Me** in the main menu on the left hand side.
        - Switch to the **My Addresses** tab. Click **+Add Address**.
        - Enter your Delegate addresses and a label (optional). Click **Submit**.
4. Complete a 2FA verification.
5. Head back to **Me** > **My Addresses** to view the Delegates you have added.
