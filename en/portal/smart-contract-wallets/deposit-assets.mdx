Note: Please make sure that you are sending tokens to the correct address and network. Any errors may result in the loss of funds.

## Deposit by assets

1. Click into a Smart Contract Wallet on Cobo Portal and switch to the **Assets** section.
2. To deposit a specific token, click the **deposit icon** next to the respective token (e.g., ETH). Please note that the tokens listed under Assets are automatically retrieved from your Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; and cannot be modified on Cobo Portal.
3. In the pop-up window, either copy the deposit address for the corresponding token or scan the QR code. If you wish to deposit from any of your wallets created on Cobo Portal, click **Deposit from other wallets** instead.
4. Following a successful deposit, the balance for the corresponding token will be automatically updated. You can switch to the Assets tab to view the updated balance.

## Deposit directly into Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;
1. To make a deposit directly into your Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;, click the **deposit icon** next to **Total Balance** instead. In the pop-up window, either copy your Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; address or scan the QR code.
2. Following a successful deposit, the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; balance will be automatically updated on Cobo Portal.
