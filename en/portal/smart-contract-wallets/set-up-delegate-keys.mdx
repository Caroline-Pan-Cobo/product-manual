The Delegate Key is used to initiate, but not sign, multi-sig transactions on behalf of Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signers. It is a dedicated address created on Cobo Portal and is required for creating on-chain transaction policies. Follow the instructions below to set up your Delegate Keys.

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_wallet.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Smart Contract Wallets** > <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_delegate key.svg" className="icon"></img> next to the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; you want to use.
3. Click <img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/button_copy.svg" className="icon"></img> to copy the link shown in the pop-up window. Then, send it to any of your Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signers to sign. Alternatively, click **Copy All and Close** to copy all information, including the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; address, Delegate Key, and URL.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/4.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>
4. (For Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signer) Navigate to the link provided and connect to your wallet associated with the Safe&#123;Wallet&#125;.
5. (For Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signer) In the pop-up window, review the auto-filled fields and enter a label for the Delegate Key. Then, click **Sign Message**.
<img src="/en/images/smart-contract-wallet/5.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>
6. (For Safe&#123;Wallet&#125; signer) Sign a message in your connected wallet.
7. The new Delegate Key will be displayed on the homepage.
