Staking is a [Cobo Portal App](/en/portal/portal-apps/introduction) developed by Cobo. To install Staking, follow the instructions in [Browse and install apps](/en/portal/portal-apps/browse-and-install).

<Info>The Staking app supports staking BTC (Babylon) and ETH (Beacon Chain) within [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/introduction) in [Organization-Controlled Vaults](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/create-mpc-vault) and [Web3 Wallets](/en/portal/custodial-wallets/web3-wallets/set-up).</Info>
