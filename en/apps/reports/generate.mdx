<Info>Prerequisite: Ensure you have the [admin or operator](/en/portal/organization/roles-and-permissions) role.</Info>

### Transaction Reports

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/reports/apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/>, find Reports, then click **Open** to open Reports.
3. Make sure you're on the **Transactions** tab.
4. Click **Generate**.
5. Set the **Reporting Period**:
   - Choose a timezone (for example, UTC+8)
   - Set the timeframe (for example, from 2024-09-09 1:00 PM to 2024-09-10 3:00 PM)
6. Specify the **Wallet Scope**:
   - Select all wallets for auditing purposes, or
   - Choose one or more specific wallets for targeted reporting
7. Check the **Password** box if you want to enable password protection.
8. Click **Generate** to create the report.
9. You can view the status of your reports in the **Report Logs** section, including those that are generated, in progress, or failed.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/reports/3.png" className="full_screen" alt="Image showing Reports as a Cobo Portal App."/>

### Asset Holdings Reports

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login)ã€‚
2. Click <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/reports/apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/>, find Reports, then click **Open** to open Reports.
3. Switch to the **Asset Holdings** tab.
4. Click **Generate**.
5. Set the **Reporting Time**:
   - Choose a timezone (for example, UTC+8)
   - Define the reporting time (for example, 1PM on 2024-09-09)
   <Info>The earliest timestamp you can select is midnight on September 2, 2024.</Info>
6. Select the report format: **By Wallets** or By **Assets**.
7. Specify the **Wallet Scope**:
   - Select all wallets for auditing purposes, or
   - Choose one or more specific wallets for targeted reporting
8. Check the **Password** box if you want to enable password protection.
9. Click **Generate** to create the report.
10. You can view the status of your reports in the **Report Logs** section, including those that are generated, in progress, or failed.
