<Info>Prerequisite: Ensure you have the [admin or operator](/en/portal/organization/roles-and-permissions) role.</Info>

### Transaction Reports

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/reports/apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/>, find Reports, then click **Open** to open Reports.
3. Switch to the Transactions tab.
4. Select a timezone for your report. For example, UTC+8.
5. Select the timeframe for your report. For instance, from 1PM on 2024-09-09 to 3PM on 2024-09-10.
6. Select one or more wallets for which you want to view transactions.
7. Click **Generate**.
8. You can view the status of your reports, including those that are generated, in progress, or failed.

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/reports/3.png" className="full_screen" alt="Image showing Reports as a Cobo Portal App."/>

### Asset Holdings Reports

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login)ã€‚
2. Click <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/reports/apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/>, find Reports, then click **Open** to open Reports.
3. Switch to the Asset Holdings tab.
4. Select a timezone for your report. For example, UTC+8.
5. Select the timestamp for your report. For example, 1PM on 2024-09-09. This means your asset balances will be captured at this specific point in time.
-  Please note that the earliest timestamp you can select is midnight on September 2, 2024.
6. Select one or more wallets for which you want to view balances.
7. Click **Generate**.
8. You can view the status of your reports, including those that are generated, in progress, or failed.
