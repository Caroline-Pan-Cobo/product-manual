Reports is a [Cobo Portal App](/en/apps/introduction) developed by Cobo.  It provides a clear overview of your asset allocation across Custodial Wallets and MPC Wallets, along with your transaction history for a specified timeframe.

## Key features
### Professional use cases
Export reports for purposes such as tax reporting, reconciliation, and submitting financial documentation to regulatory entities or other institutions.

### Enhanced security
Safeguard sensitive data with custom passwords for your online and PDF reports, limiting access to only authorized users.

### Standardized Format
Reports are exported in a standardized, user-friendly PDF format, ensuring complete audit trails and data accuracy.

### Secure and Convenient Sharing
Share via PDF download or online link, with download option available in online viewer. Password protection, if enabled when generating reports, applies to all access methods.
