Reports is a [Cobo Portal App](/en/portal/portal-apps/introduction) developed by Cobo.  It provides a clear overview of your asset allocation across Custodial Wallets and MPC Wallets, along with your transaction history for a specified timeframe.
## Key features
### Professional Use Cases
Export reports for purposes such as tax reporting, reconciliation, and submitting financial documentation to regulatory entities or other institutions.
### Standardized Formatting
Export reports in a standardized, user-friendly PDF format to ensure the preservation of audit trails and maintain data integrity.
