## Overview

The Bridge feature of the [Token Swap app](/en/apps/token-swap/introduction) is a secure and fast cross-chain liquidity solution, enabling hassle-free token transfers across blockchains within your [Custodial Wallets (Asset Wallets)](/en/portal/custodial-wallets/introduction).

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/bridge/bridge.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Image showing the Bridge feature of the Token Swap app." />

## Use cases

Bridge is especially useful for the following scenarios:
- Moving assets to chains with higher yields or lower fees
- Consolidating fragmented assets into a single network
- Accessing chain-specific DeFi protocols and opportunities

## Key features

### Cross-chain liquidity at your fingertips
Simplify your asset transfers across blockchains within [Cobo Portal](/en/portal/introduction), with zero asset security or counter-party risks.

#### One-click solution
Manage all transactions directly within Cobo Portal—simply submit your request, and we will take care of the rest.

#### Regulatory compliance
Transactions are completed through our vetted, fully regulated vendors, ensuring compliance and protection against illicit funds.

### Built for enterprises

#### Extensive network coverage
Bridge digital assets like USDC, USDT, BTC, and ETH across top chains including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Tron, and more.

#### Speed meets simplicity
Faster-than-average cross-chain transfers through an intuitive, user-friendly interface.

#### Secure custodial environment
Easily monitor and manage assets across networks — all within Cobo's secure custodial setup.

#### WBTC merchant services
As an official WBTC merchant, Cobo facilitates BTC-to-WBTC conversions at any scale with unmatched efficiency.
