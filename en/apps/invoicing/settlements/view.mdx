1. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/> on [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login), find **Invoicing**, then click **Open** to open Invoicing.

2. On the **Settlements** page, you can view the list of your settlement requests. You can search by **Settlement Request ID**.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/invoicing/settlements.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Settlements page"/>

    Click **Last Settled Amount** to view the details of the last settled request. 

    Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_view.svg" className="icon" alt="the View icon"/> under **Actions** to view the details of a settlement request, as shown in the following screenshot.

    <img src="/en/images/portal-apps/invoicing/settlement_details.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Settlements pdetailsage"/>
