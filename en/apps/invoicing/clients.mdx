Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/> on [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login), find **Invoicing**, then click **Open** to open Invoicing. On the **Clients** page, you can view the list of your clients, and add and manage clients.

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/invoicing/clients.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Clients"/>

### Add a client

1. On the **Clients** page, click **Add Client**.
2. Fill in the required information, including **Client Email**, **Client Type** (**Enterprise** or **Personal**), **Client Name**, then click **Add Client**. 
3. If you successfully add the client, the client status will be set to **Active** automatically.

### Manage clients

You can edit client information, freeze active clients, and unfreeze frozen clients.

- Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_edit.svg" className="icon" alt="the edit icon"></img> to edit client information.
- Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_freeze.svg" className="icon" alt="the freeze icon"></img> to freeze a client that is in the **Active** status. This will restrict all invoice-related operations for the client.

    <Info>You cannot freeze a client if there is an in-progress invoice.</Info>

- Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_unfreeze.svg" className="icon" alt="the unfreeze icon"></img> to unfreeze a client that is in the **Frozen** status. This will resume all invoice-related operations for this client.
