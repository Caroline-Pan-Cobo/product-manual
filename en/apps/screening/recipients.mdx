<Info>
To access this feature:
1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login)
2. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/> to open Cobo Portal Apps
3. Find and open **Screening**
4. Click **Screening Results**
</Info> 

The notification recipients feature ensures that your team stays informed about important screening events through email notifications. Maintaining an up-to-date list of recipients is crucial for effective risk monitoring and compliance management.


## Managing notification recipients

<Note>It is a good idea to include your Risk Management and Compliance teams as notification recipients to ensure proper oversight of screening activities.</Note>
