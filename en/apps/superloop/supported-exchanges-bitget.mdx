## Funds mirrored from SuperLoop to Biget
SuperLoop mirrors your funds to a spot account (main account) in [Bitget](https://www.bitget.com/). Once credited, you can transfer the funds to other supported trading accounts within Bitget, allowing you to trade Spot, Spot Margin (in Isolated or Cross Margin mode), and various contracts, including USDT-M/USDC-M Futures, Coin-M Perpetual Futures, and Coin-M Delivery Futures.
