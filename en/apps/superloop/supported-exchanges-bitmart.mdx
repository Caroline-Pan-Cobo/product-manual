## Funds mirrored from SuperLoop to BitMart
SuperLoop mirrors your funds to a spot account (main account) in [BitMart](https://www.bitmart.com/). Once credited, you can trade spot on BitMart (contract trading will be supported in the future).

## Settlement details

- Settlement mode: Off-chain settlement
- Settlement time (UTC+8): 12:00:00

## Supported tokens & chains for BitMart when using SuperLoop
| **Token Name** | **Chain Name** |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| BTC                            | Bitcoin |
| ETH                            | Ethereum |
| USDC                           | Binance Smart Chain |
| USDT                           | Binance Smart Chain |
