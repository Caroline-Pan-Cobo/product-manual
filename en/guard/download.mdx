---
title: Download the Cobo Guard app
---

Currently, Cobo Guard only supports the iOS system. The app is compatible with iPhone 6s and newer models, and the operating system must be iOS 13.0 or later. You can download Cobo Guard on the App Store. Alternatively, simply [click here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/cobo-guard/id6450997458?ref=cobo-institutional-digital-asset-custody-provider) or scan the QR code below to download it.

<img src="/en/images/cobo-guard/common/QRcode.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>

After Cobo Guard is downloaded, open the app and set up a PIN code. To learn more about setting up Cobo Guard, refer to [Manage the Cobo Guard app](/en/guard/manage), [Generate and back up your key shares](/en/guard/generate-and-back-up-key-shares) and [Reset Cobo Guard for device change or reinstallation](/en/guard/restore-key-shares-from-icloud).
