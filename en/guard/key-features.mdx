## A transaction approval application
Use Cobo Guard to approve critical operations such as transactions and modifications to your risk management strategies.

## A key share manager
Securely generate and back up your key shares, and effortlessly restore them from iCloud when necessary.

## A Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) method for Cobo Account
Use Cobo Guard as an MFA method to protect important operations such as password changes.

## A passwordless login mechanism for Cobo Account
Unlock a passwordless login experience once you habe configured Cobo Guard on Cobo Accounts.
