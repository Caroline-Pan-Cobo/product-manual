Easily manage your Cobo Guard app by tapping the corresponding buttons:
{/*
<br />


 <div class="container">
    <p class="text">- **My Accounts**</p>
    <img src="/images/cobo-guard/manage-the-cobo-guard-app/icon-my-accounts.png" class="icon"> </img>
    <p class="text">:</p>
</div>
*/}

- **My Accounts**

View and manage the Cobo Accounts linked to your Cobo Guard. **Note**: Key share information will only be visible if you hold any key share.

{/*
<br />

 <div class="container">
    <p class="text">- **My PubKey**</p>
    <img src="/images/cobo-guard/manage-the-cobo-guard-app/icon-my-pubkey.png" class="icon"> </img>
    <p class="text">:</p>
</div>

*/}

- **My PubKey**

View and copy your Auth Key and TSS Node ID. **Note**: The TSS Node ID tab will not appear if there are no Cobo Accounts linked to your Cobo Guard.

{/*
<br />

 <div class="container">
    <p class="text">- **Test Messages**</p>
    <img src="/images/cobo-guard/manage-the-cobo-guard-app/icon-test-messages.png" class="icon"> </img>
    <p class="text">:</p>
</div>
*/}

- **Test Messages**

Send and receive test approval messages.

{/*
<br />

 <div class="container">
    <p class="text">- **Scan QR Code**</p>
    <img src="/images/cobo-guard/manage-the-cobo-guard-app/icon-scan-qr-code.png" class="icon"> </img>
    <p class="text">:</p>
</div>

*/}

- **Scan QR Code**

Quickly link your Cobo Accounts with Cobo Guard by scanning designated QR codes. Once configured on your Cobo Accounts, Cobo Guard enables a passwordless login experience.

{/*
<br />

 <div class="container">
    <p class="text">- **Pending / All Request Tabs**</p>
    <p class="text">:</p>
</div>

*/}

- **Pending / All Request Tabs**

Efficiently manage and track your approval requests. The Pending tab displays all approval requests awaiting your decision, while the All tab shows all approval requests regardless of their status.
