Easily manage your Cobo Guard app by tapping the corresponding buttons:

- **My Accounts** <img src="/en/images/cobo-guard/common/icon_account.svg" className="icon"></img>

View and manage the Cobo Accounts linked to your Cobo Guard. **Note**: Key share information will only be visible if you hold any key share.

- **My Public Key** <img src="/en/images/cobo-guard/common/icon_key.svg" className="icon"></img>

View and copy your Public Key and TSS Node ID. **Note**: The TSS Node ID tab will not appear if there are no Cobo Accounts linked to your Cobo Guard.

- **Test Messages** <img src="/en/images/cobo-guard/common/icon_testmsg.svg" className="icon"></img>

Send and receive test approval messages.

- **Scan QR Code** <img src="/en/images/cobo-guard/common/icon_scan.svg" className="icon"></img>

Quickly link your Cobo Accounts with Cobo Guard by scanning designated QR codes. Once configured on your Cobo Accounts, Cobo Guard enables a passwordless login experience.


- **Pending / All Request Tabs** <img src="/en/images/organization/nav.svg" className="icon"></img>

Efficiently manage and track your approval requests. The Pending tab displays all approval requests awaiting your decision, while the All tab shows all approval requests regardless of their status.
